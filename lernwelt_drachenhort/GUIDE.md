# Player Guide — Drachenhort (Dragon Hoard) 🐉🥚

This guide explains **all the elements** of the "Drachenhort" learning world, **how to use
them** and **what the tasks and goals are**. Made for **parents and children playing together**
(about 5–7 years old).

> Note: the in-game texts are in **German** (the theme is authored in German), so names you
> see on screen are German; this guide adds English explanations.

> Motto: **Ausbrüten · Umsorgen · Anfreunden** (Hatch · Care · Befriend)
>
> There is no "winning" — the point is to discover, care and learn: befriend and photograph
> dragons, solve little tasks, hatch an egg and patiently raise a baby dragon, while practising
> counting and matching colours (Swiss curriculum *Lehrplan 21*, cycle 1).

---

## 1. Quick start

1. Make a new world with **VoxeLibre** or **Mineclonia** and enable the mods
   **`lernwelt`** + **`lernwelt_drachenhort`**. (Tips for a mountainous world:
   **[WELT-EINRICHTEN.md](WELT-EINRICHTEN.md)**.)
2. Enter the world → you automatically get the **starter kit**.
3. Fast start: type the command **`/drachenhort_nest`** — a ready-made dragon nest with boards,
   eggs, a hatching egg and a riding dragon appears in front of you.

---

## 2. Basic controls

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| **Use / befriend a dragon / feed a baby / open a board** | **Right-click** |
| **Photograph / dig blocks** | **Left-click** (hold) |
| **Build / place an item (egg, block …)** | **Right-click** on the ground |
| **Fly (creative)** | Double-jump; then jump = up, sneak = down |

In the child setting, **damage is off** — nothing bad can happen. The time of day is fixed (no
scary night), and there are no monsters. The crater's "cold lava" is **completely harmless**.

---

## 3. The world: 3 learning zones

Each zone is a "habitat" with a **learning board** and matching dragons:

| Zone | Dragons there | Learning areas |
|---|---|---|
| 🪨 **Drachenberg** (dragon mountain) | Rock, Emerald, Crystal dragon | Maths (counting), science, colours |
| 🌋 **Lavafreier Krater** (lava-free crater) | Ember, Spark, Berry dragon | Science, maths (counting) |
| ☁️ **Himmelsinseln** (sky islands) | Cloud, Wind, Sun dragon, *Star dragon* | Science (care/patience), colours |

> The crater is **warm but safe**: the lava is **cooled** ("kalte Lava") and does nothing.

---

## 4. Learning boards & tasks (the learning heart)

**Use:** right-click a learning board → a window shows the **activity**, the **curriculum
links** and the zone's **tasks**.

Three task types:

- ❓ **Quiz** — a question with answer buttons. Right = ✓ + reward, wrong = "try again".
- 🎨 **Copy the pattern / match colours** — a colour pattern (e.g. red-yellow-red) is shown at
  the top. Click the colour buttons **in the right order**. A mistake resets the pattern.
- 🐉 **Befriend goal** — e.g. "befriend 2 rock dragons". The progress (x/N) counts up
  automatically.

Solved tasks give a sound + praise and count towards your progress (see `/lernfortschritt`).

**Which tasks are there?**

| Zone | Tasks |
|---|---|
| Dragon mountain | **Count eggs** (3) · **match colours** red-yellow-red · befriend 2 rock dragons |
| Lava-free crater | Quiz "ember dragon colour?" · **count eggs** (4) · befriend 1 ember dragon |
| Sky islands | Quiz "colour of a cloud?" · **match colours** blue-white-blue · Quiz "what does a baby need?" |

## 5. Raising a baby dragon 🥚🐉 (responsibility & patience)

The **heart** of the theme. Care for your own baby dragon step by step:

1. 🥚 **Hatch an egg:** hold a **Brut-Ei** (hatching egg) and place it on the ground (best in a
   **nest**). It is warm and glows. After a short time (about **30 s**) a baby dragon
   **hatches** in a random colour.
2. 🍓 **Feed it:** hold **Drachenfutter** (dragon food / berries) and **right-click** the baby.
   It is happy (hearts + sound).
3. ⏳ **Be patient:** after each feeding the baby is **full** for a while. Feed too fast and it
   says "I'm still full — be patient." **Wait a moment**, then feed again. (This is exactly how
   the game practises **patience**.)
4. 🤝 **Tame it:** after **5 patient feedings** the baby is **tamed** — it **trusts you**, grows
   a little, glows softly and **follows you** around. It now belongs to you (the classic
   animal-mod pattern: tame by feeding, with an owner).

Each tamed baby counts in your tally ("Gezähmte Drachenbabys: N"). A hungry baby sometimes
shows a small heart above its head — then it wants food again.

> No mob API? **No problem:** hatching eggs, baby dragons, feeding and taming are the theme's
> own code and always work.

---

## 6. Counting eggs & matching colours 🔢🎨

- 🔢 **Counting eggs:** there are **dragon eggs in 5 colours** (`ei_rot/blau/gruen/gelb/lila`)
  to place. Lay some in a nest and **count** them together — just like the board tasks (fits
  **MA.1: counting**). The command `/drachenhort_nest` lays out a clutch of eggs to count.
- 🎨 **Matching colours:** the boards have **pattern tasks** — tap the colours in the right
  order. You can also lay out and sort your own colour rows with the eggs and dragon stones.

---

## 7. Befriending dragons 🐲

**Use:** right-click a dragon = **befriend**. You get hearts, a sound, a counter entry and
(after enough) a new rank. All dragons are **peaceful** — no fighting.

**All dragons:**

| Dragon (DE / EN) | Home | Eats | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| Glutdrache / Ember dragon | Crater | Ember berries | 👪 family |
| Funkendrache / Spark dragon | Crater | Berries | nimble |
| Beerendrache / Berry dragon | Crater | Sweet berries | 👪 family, tiny |
| Felsendrache / Rock dragon | Mountain | Mountain herbs | big & strong |
| Smaragddrache / Emerald dragon | Mountain | Mountain herbs | shiny green |
| Kristalldrache / Crystal dragon | Mountain | Crystals | 💡 glows |
| Wolkendrache / Cloud dragon | Sky islands | Cloud fluff | 👪 family, light |
| Winddrache / Wind dragon | Sky islands | Wind berries | very fast |
| Sonnendrache / Sun dragon | Sky islands | Sunbeams | 💡 glows |
| **Sternendrache / Star dragon** | Sky islands | Stardust | 🌟 very rare, glows |

- 👪 **Families:** befriend a parent (Ember, Berry, Cloud) and its **babies** appear, which you
  can befriend too.
- 🌟 **Star dragon:** does **not** appear by itself (very rare). Get it from its **spawn egg**
  (in the starter kit / creative inventory).
- 🌍 **Living world:** the other dragons **appear automatically** on land near you (gently
  capped). Can be turned off (see Settings).

**Spawn eggs:** every dragon has an egg. Egg in hand → **right-click** the ground = place the
dragon. (Note: these are the **mob** spawn eggs — not the colourful **counting eggs** or the
**hatching egg**.)

---

## 8. Explorer camera 📷

**Use:** hold the camera, get near a dragon and **left-click** = photo. The nearest
**not-yet-discovered** dragon is marked **discovered** in the diary (range about 12 blocks).

This is the **collecting game**: photograph **all** dragons. The diary shows "Discovered:
X/10". Discovering is separate from befriending.

---

## 9. Dragon diary 📋

**Use:** diary in hand → **right-click** to open. You see a list of all dragons; click one for
the **profile** (name, habitat, eats, power), how **often you befriended** it, and whether it
has been **discovered**. The top shows your **rank**, the **total befriended** and the
**discovered count**.

---

## 10. Ranks & badges 🏆

Every befriending counts. At certain thresholds your rank goes up (sound + pop-up); from
"Drachenreiter" on you also get a **badge item** (right-click it for your tally).

| From … befriended | Rank | Badge |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | Eierhüter (Egg keeper) | – |
| 5 | Drachenfreund (Dragon friend) | – |
| 10 | Drachenreiter (Dragon rider) | 🥉 yes |
| 25 | Drachenhüter (Dragon guardian) | 🥈 yes |
| 50 | Drachenmeister (Dragon master) | 🥇 yes |

Top-left a **HUD** always shows: world · rank · befriended.

---

## 11. Riding dragon 🐉

**Use:**
1. **Place** the riding-dragon item (right-click on the ground).
2. **Right-click** the dragon = **mount**.
3. Fly: **W/S** forward/back · **A/D** steer · **jump** = up · **sneak** = down ·
   **right-click** = dismount.

**Features:**
- ✨ **Spark trail** — warm sparks while flying.
- 💡 **Glow** — the dragon glows while someone rides.
- 👨‍👧 **Second seat** — a **second player** can **fly along** with right-click (ideal for
  parent + child, great for the sky islands).

## 12. Blocks, items & internal names

Everything is generated without external pictures. Internal names (for the creative search or
`/giveme`) begin with `lernwelt_drachenhort:`.

**Blocks:** `drachenstein_rot/blau/gruen/gelb/lila` (glowing dragon stones) · `bergstein` ·
`wolkenblock` · `kristallglas` (transparent) · `kalte_lava` (glowing but harmless) · `nest`
(a real dragon nest with a straw rim on top) · `goldhort` (gold heap) ·
`tafel_berg/krater/himmelsinseln` (learning boards).

**Eggs:** `ei_rot/blau/gruen/gelb/lila` (colourful **counting eggs**) · `brutei` (warm
**hatching egg** — hatches into a baby dragon).

**Patterned build blocks:** `schuppen_rot`, `schuppen_gruen` (dragon scales), `drachenauge`
(dragon eye), `ei_muster`, `berg_muster`, `rahmen_gold`, and `buchstabe_a` … `buchstabe_z`
(**letter blocks A–Z** to lay out words). In creative mode all blocks are in the build menu.

**Items:** `logbuch` (diary) · `kamera` · `reitdrache` · `drachenfutter` (berries) · the mob
spawn eggs (e.g. `glutdrache`, `wolkendrache`, `sternendrache`) · badges (`badge_10/25/50`).

> **Own patterns or pictures on blocks?** Yes! A block can use `texture = "..."` instead of
> `color = "#..."`:
> - **Pattern without a file** — layer colour rectangles, e.g.
>   `texture = "[fill:16x16:#7f8c8d^[fill:8x8:0,0:#566573^[fill:8x8:8,8:#566573"` (checker).
>   Overlay format: `^[fill:WIDTHxHEIGHT:X,Y:#colour`.
> - **A real picture** — put a PNG (16×16 or 32×32) in the `textures/` folder and set
>   `texture = "lernwelt_drachenhort_mypic.png"`.
> - **Per face** — `top`, `side` and `bottom` give a block different faces (like the nest).
> See `textures/README.txt`.

## 14. Settings

In the menu under **Settings → All settings → Mods** (or in `minetest.conf`):

| Setting | Default | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| `lernwelt_show_hud` | on | Progress display top-left |
| `lernwelt_spawn_creatures` | on | Wild dragons appear automatically on land |
| `lernwelt_big_text` | off | **Larger text** on the learning boards |
| `lernwelt_easy_mode` | off | **Easy mode**: quizzes show only 2 answers |
| `lernwelt_drachenhort_starter_kit` | on | Starter kit on first join |

## 15. For parents & teachers 👪

- **Lehrplan 21 (cycle 1):** every zone and task is tagged with competence areas — **Maths**
  (counting eggs, ordering patterns/colours) and **science/NMG** (animals & habitats,
  **responsibility** for an animal, practising **patience**).
- **Raising a baby dragon** is deliberately built around **responsibility and patience**: it
  needs **several feedings over time** (with a wait in between) before the baby trusts you.
- `/lernplan` shows all zones and their links at a glance.
- `/lernfortschritt` shows per child: rank, befriended, tasks solved, dragons met and the
  learning areas covered.
- **Accessibility:** `lernwelt_big_text` (large text) and `lernwelt_easy_mode` (fewer quiz
  options) make it easier to start.

## 16. Little missions & ideas

- 🥚 **Hatcher:** hatch a Brut-Ei and feed the baby until it is tamed.
- ⏳ **Patience test:** raise three babies without getting impatient.
- 🟢 **First steps:** befriend 5 dragons → rank "Drachenfreund".
- 📷 **Researcher:** photograph all dragons (Discovered 10/10).
- 🧩 **Puzzler:** solve all tasks on all three boards.
- 🔢 **Counting champ:** lay 10 eggs in a nest and count them.
- 🔤 **Word play:** lay your name with the letter blocks (`buchstabe_*`).
- 🌟 **Legend:** befriend the rare star dragon.
- 👨‍👧 **Team flight:** ride the dragon together to the sky islands.

## 17. Troubleshooting

- **Dragons don't appear automatically:** `lernwelt_spawn_creatures` must be on and a mob API
  is required (VoxeLibre/Mineclonia or `mobs_redo`). You can always place dragons with **spawn
  eggs**.
- **Egg hatches but no living dragon?** Hatching eggs, babies and feeding always work (own
  code) — only the *big, wild* dragons need a mob API.
- **Baby won't eat:** **Drachenfutter** must be **in your hand**, and the baby's "full" pause
  must be over (wait a moment). Then **right-click** the baby.
- **No sound:** sound files are optional. Put your own `.ogg` files in the `sounds/` folder
  (see `sounds/README.txt`) — without them everything works, just silently.

Have fun hatching, caring and befriending! 🐉🥚
