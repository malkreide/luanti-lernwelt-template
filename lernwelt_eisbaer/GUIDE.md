# Player Guide — Eisbär-Bucht (Polar Bay) 🐻‍❄️❄️

This guide explains **all the elements** of the "Eisbär-Bucht" learning world, **how to use
them** and **what the tasks and goals are**. Made for **parents and children playing together**
(about 6–9 years old).

> Note: the in-game texts are in **German** (the theme is authored in German), so names you see
> on screen are German; this guide adds English explanations.

> Motto: **Entdecken · Benennen · Bewahren** (Discover · Name · Protect)
>
> There is no "winning" — the point is to discover, name and care: find cold animals and match
> them to their **habitat**, feed animals, watch the **aurora** and lay out **blue-white
> patterns**, while practising **animals & habitats** (Swiss curriculum *Lehrplan 21*, NMG.2).

---

## 1. Quick start

1. Make a new world with **VoxeLibre** or **Mineclonia** and enable the mods
   **`lernwelt`** + **`lernwelt_eisbaer`**. (World tips:
   **[WELT-EINRICHTEN.md](WELT-EINRICHTEN.md)**.)
2. Enter the world → you automatically get the **starter kit**.
3. Fast start: type **`/eisbaer_eispalast`** — a whole ice palace with learning boards, an
   aurora wall, a sled and a feeding station appears. Or **`/eisbaer_iglu`** for a small igloo.

---

## 2. Basic controls

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| **Use / help an animal / feed at the station / open a board / board the sled** | **Right-click** |
| **Photograph / dig blocks** | **Left-click** (hold) |
| **Build / place an item (block, sled, sapling …)** | **Right-click** on the ground |
| **Fly (creative)** | Double-jump; then jump = up, sneak = down |

In the child setting, **damage is off** — nothing bad can happen, and there is **no freezing**.
The time of day is fixed (bright snow world), and there are no monsters. All animals are peaceful.

---

## 3. The world: 3 learning zones (habitats)

Each zone is a **habitat** with a **learning board** and matching animals:

| Zone | Animals there | Learning areas |
|---|---|---|
| 🧊 **Gefrorener See** (frozen lake) | Penguin, seal, walrus, beluga, narwhal | Animals & habitats, counting |
| 🌲 **Schneewald** (snow forest) | Snow hare, arctic fox, reindeer, snowy owl | Animals & habitats, naming, patterns |
| 🌌 **Polarlicht-Höhle** (aurora cave) | Polar bear, aurora fox, *aurora bear* | Light & dark, colours & patterns |

---

## 4. Learning boards & tasks (the learning heart)

**Use:** right-click a learning board → a window shows the **activity**, the **curriculum
links** and the zone's **tasks**.

Three task types:

- ❓ **Quiz** — a question with answer buttons. Right = ✓ + reward, wrong = "try again".
- 🎨 **Copy the pattern** — a **blue-white pattern** is shown at the top. Click the buttons **in
  the right order**. A mistake resets it. This practises **patterns and colours**.
- 🐾 **Help goal** — e.g. "help 2 penguins". The progress (x/N) counts up automatically.

Solved tasks give a sound + praise and count towards your progress (see `/lernfortschritt`).

---

## 5. Helping animals 🐾

**Use:** right-click an animal = **help**. You get hearts, a sound, a counter entry and (after
enough) a new rank. All animals are **peaceful** — no fighting.

**All animals:**

| Animal (DE / EN) | Habitat | "Eats" | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pinguin / Penguin | Frozen lake | Fish | waddles & slides |
| Robbe / Seal | Frozen lake | Fish | 🏊 swims · 👪 helpers |
| Walross / Walrus | Frozen lake | Shells | big · 👪 helpers |
| Beluga-Wal / Beluga whale | Frozen lake | Fish | 🏊 white, singing whale |
| Narwal / Narwhal | Frozen lake | Fish | 🏊 "unicorn of the sea" |
| Schneehase / Snow hare | Snow forest | Bark & moss | nimble · 👪 helpers |
| Polarfuchs / Arctic fox | Snow forest | Small animals | white camouflage fur |
| Rentier / Reindeer | Snow forest | Lichen | big antlers · 👪 helpers |
| Schneeeule / Snowy owl | Snow forest | Mice | flies silently |
| Eisbär / Polar bear | Aurora cave | Seals & fish | big & strong · 👪 helpers |
| Polarlicht-Fuchs / Aurora fox | Aurora cave | Stardust | 💡 glows |
| **Polarlicht-Bär / Aurora bear** | Aurora cave | Aurora light | 🌟 very rare, glows |

- 👪 **Families:** help a "parent" animal (seal, walrus, snow hare, reindeer, polar bear) and
  small helpers appear, which you can help too.
- 🏊 **Water animals** (seal, beluga, narwhal) appear **in water**, the others **on land** near
  you (gently capped, can be turned off).
- 🌟 **Aurora bear:** does **not** appear by itself (very rare). Get it from its **spawn egg**
  (in the starter kit / creative inventory).

**Spawn eggs:** every animal has an egg. Egg in hand → **right-click** the ground = place it.

---

## 6. Feeding animals 🐟 (caring)

1. 🧰 Place a **Futterstelle** (feeding station) on the ground.
2. 🐟 Hold a **Fisch** (fish) and **right-click** the station → the animals are fed.
3. ⭐ Each feed counts ("Satte Tiere: N"). At 5, 15 and 30 you get a little award
   (Tier-Freund → Polar-Pfleger → Kälte-Kümmerer).

The lesson: **caring** for animals and taking responsibility (protect).

---

## 7. The aurora (Polarlicht) 🌌

The **Polarlicht** is a glowing block that **slowly cycles colour** (blue → teal → green →
violet → white → …). Place several side by side to make a glowing **aurora wall** — especially
pretty in the aurora cave or the igloo. A gentle lesson in **colours and patterns** (light & dark).

---

## 8. Blue-white patterns 🎨

The theme has five **blue-white pattern blocks**: **stripes**, **dots**, **checkerboard**,
**waves** and **snowflakes**. Use them to decorate the igloo/palace, to copy the **pattern
tasks** at the boards, or grow them from **ice flowers** (see below).

---

## 9. Planting ice flowers 🌸

**Use:** place an **Eisblumen-Setzling** (ice-flower sapling). After a while it **grows** into a
random **blue-white pattern block**. Each grown flower counts ("Muster-Blöcke: N").

---

## 10. The sled 🛷

**Use:** place a **Schlitten** (sled), then **right-click** to board. **W/S** drive, **A/D**
steer, **jump** = a small hop, **right-click** = get off. A **second player** can ride along!
The sled glides over snow and ice — great for exploring the whole bay.

---

## 11. Explorer camera 📷

**Use:** hold the camera, get near an animal and **left-click** = photo. The nearest
**not-yet-discovered** animal is marked **discovered** in the logbook (range about 12 blocks).
Photograph **all** animals; the logbook shows "Discovered: X/12". Separate from helping.

---

## 12. Polar logbook 📋

**Use:** logbook in hand → **right-click** to open. You see a list of all animals; click one for
the **profile** (name, **habitat**, "eats", note), how **often you helped** it, and whether it
has been **discovered**. The top shows your **rank**, the **total helped** and the **discovered
count**.

---

## 13. Ranks & badges 🏆

Every help counts. At certain thresholds your rank goes up (sound + pop-up); from "Polar-Forscher"
on you also get a **badge item** (right-click it for your tally).

| From … helped | Rank | Badge |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | Schnee-Frischling (snow rookie) | – |
| 5 | Spurenleser (tracker) | – |
| 10 | Polar-Forscher (polar researcher) | 🥉 yes |
| 25 | Eisbär-Freund (polar-bear friend) | 🥈 yes |
| 50 | Polar-Held (polar hero) | 🥇 yes |

Top-left a **HUD** always shows: world · rank · helped.

---

## 14. Blocks, items & internal names

Everything is generated without external pictures. Internal names (for the creative search or
`/giveme`) begin with `lernwelt_eisbaer:`.

**Snow & ice:** `schnee` · `packeis` · `blaueis` · `klareis` (transparent) · `iglu_block` ·
`eisziegel` · `fels_schnee` · `eiskristall` (glowing) · `lagerfeuer` (warm, glowing) ·
`proviantkiste` (per-face).

**Blue-white patterns:** `muster_streifen` · `muster_punkte` · `muster_schach` · `muster_welle` ·
`schneeflocke_block`.

**Aurora:** `polarlicht` (glows & cycles colour; the colour variants appear automatically and
are not in the creative menu).

**Caring:** `futterstelle` (feed with fish) · `fisch` (food) · `eisblume_setzling` (grows into a
pattern block).

**Letter blocks:** `buchstabe_a` … `buchstabe_z` (A–Z) — for labelling (lay out animal names).
For Umlauts use AE/OE/UE.

**Items:** `logbuch` (polar logbook) · `kamera` · `schlitten` (sled) · the mob spawn eggs (e.g.
`pinguin`, `eisbaer`, `polarlicht_baer`) · badges (`badge_10/25/50`) · the learning boards
(`tafel_gefrorener_see` / `tafel_schneewald` / `tafel_polarlicht_hoehle`).

> **Own patterns or pictures on blocks?** Yes! A block can use `texture = "..."` instead of
> `color = "#..."` (a pattern without a file, a PNG, or per face `top/side/bottom`). See
> `textures/README.txt`.

## 16. Settings

In the menu under **Settings → All settings → Mods** (or in `minetest.conf`):

| Setting | Default | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| `lernwelt_show_hud` | on | Progress display top-left |
| `lernwelt_spawn_creatures` | on | Animals appear automatically (land & water) |
| `lernwelt_big_text` | off | **Larger text** on the learning boards |
| `lernwelt_easy_mode` | off | **Easy mode**: quizzes show only 2 answers |
| `lernwelt_eisbaer_starter_kit` | on | Starter kit on first join |

## 17. For parents & teachers 👪

- **Lehrplan 21 (NMG.2 – animals & habitats):** every zone and task is tagged with competence
  areas — **science/NMG** (animals and their habitats, light & dark / the aurora),
  **mathematics** (counting, ordering patterns & sizes), **German** (naming animals),
  **movement** (moving safely on ice & snow) and **art** (blue-white patterns).
- **Matching animal to habitat** (who lives in water, on land, in the cave) is the core of
  NMG.2 — the logbook shows each animal's **habitat**.
- **Feeding** and **helping** make **responsibility & care** tangible (protect).
- `/lernplan` shows all zones and their links at a glance; `/lernfortschritt` shows per child:
  rank, helped, tasks solved, animals met and learning areas covered.
- **Accessibility:** `lernwelt_big_text` (large text) and `lernwelt_easy_mode` (fewer quiz
  options) make it easier to start.

## 18. Little missions & ideas

- 🐧 **First help:** help 2 penguins at the frozen lake.
- 🐻‍❄️ **King of the ice:** find and help the big polar bear in the aurora cave.
- 🌌 **Aurora wall:** build a row of aurora blocks and watch them change colour.
- 🎨 **Pattern artist:** lay out your own blue-white pattern (stripes, dots, checkerboard).
- 🌸 **Ice garden:** plant 5 ice flowers.
- 🐟 **Animal carer:** feed animals until you are "Tier-Freund" (5×).
- 🛷 **Sled ride:** ride the sled across the bay (with two players!).
- 📷 **Researcher:** photograph all animals (Discovered 12/12).
- 🌟 **Legend:** find the rare aurora bear.
- 🔤 **Animal sign:** spell an animal name with letter blocks (e.g. EISBAER).

## 19. Troubleshooting

- **Animals don't appear automatically:** `lernwelt_spawn_creatures` must be on and a mob API is
  required (VoxeLibre/Mineclonia or `mobs_redo`). You can always place animals with **spawn eggs**.
- **Water animals missing:** seal, beluga and narwhal need **water** nearby (a lake or sea). They
  do not appear on land.
- **Feeding station does nothing:** you must hold **fish** in your hand (from the starter kit or
  creative inventory).
- **Aurora doesn't change colour:** freshly placed blocks start by themselves. Use
  `/eisbaer_eispalast` for a ready-made glowing aurora wall.
- **No sound:** sound files are optional. Put your own `.ogg` files in the `sounds/` folder (see
  `sounds/README.txt`) — without them everything works, just silently.

Have fun discovering, naming and protecting! 🐻‍❄️❄️
