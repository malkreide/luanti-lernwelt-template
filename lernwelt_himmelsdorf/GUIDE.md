# Player Guide — Himmelsdorf (Cloud City) 🌈☁️🎈

This guide explains **all the elements** of the "Himmelsdorf" learning world, **how to use
them** and **what the tasks and goals are**. Made for **parents and children playing together**
(about 6–9 years old).

> Note: the in-game texts are in **German** (the theme is authored in German), so names you see
> on screen are German; this guide adds English explanations.

> Motto: **Entdecken · Benennen · Bewahren** (Discover · Name · Protect)
>
> There is no "winning" — the point is to discover, name and care: learn the seven **rainbow
> colours**, watch the **weather**, experience **up/down** and understand **flight physics**,
> while practising **weather & spatial orientation** (Swiss curriculum *Lehrplan 21*, NMG).

---

## 1. Quick start

1. Make a new world with **VoxeLibre** or **Mineclonia** and enable the mods
   **`lernwelt`** + **`lernwelt_himmelsdorf`**. (World tips:
   **[WELT-EINRICHTEN.md](WELT-EINRICHTEN.md)**.)
2. Enter the world → you automatically get the **starter kit**.
3. Fast start: type **`/himmelsdorf_wolkenstadt`** — a whole cloud city with learning boards, a
   rainbow bridge, a weather station and a balloon appears. Or **`/himmelsdorf_schwebeinsel`**
   for a small floating island.

---

## 2. Basic controls

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| **Use / help an animal / change the weather / open a board / board the balloon** | **Right-click** |
| **Photograph / measure height / dig blocks** | **Left-click** |
| **Build / place an item (block, balloon, seed …)** | **Right-click** on the ground |
| **Fly (creative)** | Double-jump; then jump = up, sneak = down |

In the child setting, **damage is off** — nothing bad can happen, and there is **no fall
damage** (perfect for a world high in the air!). The time of day is fixed (bright sky), and there
are no monsters. All animals are peaceful.

---

## 3. The world: 3 learning zones

Each zone is a sky area with a **learning board** and matching animals:

| Zone | Animals there | Learning areas |
|---|---|---|
| 🌈 **Regenbogenbrücke** (rainbow bridge) | Rainbow hummingbird, sun butterfly, sun bird, *rainbow pegasus* | Rainbow colours, patterns |
| ☁️ **Wolkenfelder** (cloud fields) | Cloud sheep, cloud cat, swallow, sky jellyfish | Weather, up/down |
| 🎈 **Ballonhafen** (balloon harbour) | Storm bird, wind sprite, weather frog, thunderbird | Flight physics, spatial orientation |

---

## 4. Learning boards & tasks (the learning heart)

**Use:** right-click a learning board → a window shows the **activity**, the **curriculum
links** and the zone's **tasks**.

Three task types:

- ❓ **Quiz** — a question with answer buttons. Right = ✓ + reward, wrong = "try again".
- 🎨 **Copy the pattern** — a **rainbow pattern** is shown at the top. Click the buttons **in
  the right order**. A mistake resets it. This practises **colours and sequences**.
- 🐾 **Help goal** — e.g. "help 2 hummingbirds". The progress (x/N) counts up automatically.

Solved tasks give a sound + praise and count towards your progress (see `/lernfortschritt`).

---

## 5. Helping animals 🐾

**Use:** right-click an animal = **help**. You get hearts, a sound, a counter entry and (after
enough) a new rank. All animals are **peaceful** — no fighting.

**All animals:**

| Animal (DE / EN) | Zone | "Eats" | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| Regenbogen-Kolibri / Rainbow hummingbird | Rainbow bridge | Flower nectar | 💡 glows, whirrs |
| Sonnenfalter / Sun butterfly | Rainbow bridge | Nectar | colourful wings |
| Sonnenvogel / Sun bird | Rainbow bridge | Sun grains | 💡 glows warm |
| **Regenbogen-Pegasus / Rainbow pegasus** | Rainbow bridge | Rainbow light | 🌟 very rare, glows |
| Wolkenschaf / Cloud sheep | Cloud fields | Cloud grass | soft · 👪 helpers |
| Wolkenkatze / Cloud cat | Cloud fields | Milk | purrs on clouds |
| Schwalbe / Swallow | Cloud fields | Midges | very fast |
| Himmelsqualle / Sky jellyfish | Cloud fields | Sun dust | 💡 floats gently |
| Sturmvogel / Storm bird | Balloon harbour | Wind | rides the storm |
| Windgeist / Wind sprite | Balloon harbour | Fresh air | 💡 whirling glow |
| Wetterfrosch / Weather frog | Balloon harbour | Flies | predicts the weather · 👪 helpers |
| Donnervogel / Thunderbird | Balloon harbour | Thunderstorms | 💡 wings sound like thunder |

- 👪 **Families:** help a "parent" animal (cloud sheep, weather frog) and small helpers appear,
  which you can help too.
- 🌍 **Animals appear** automatically on the cloud platforms / floating island near you (gently
  capped, can be turned off). They need a **solid surface** to appear on.
- 🌟 **Rainbow pegasus:** does **not** appear by itself (very rare). Get it from its **spawn egg**
  (in the starter kit / creative inventory).

**Spawn eggs:** every animal has an egg. Egg in hand → **right-click** the ground = place it.

---

## 6. The weather station 🌦️

**Use:** place a **Wetter-Station** (weather station) and **right-click** it → the weather
changes for **all** players:

**Sun → Clouds → Rain → Thunderstorm → Rainbow → (sun again)**

The **sky** recolours, the **clouds** get denser or lighter, **rain** and **thunderstorm** drop
**droplets**, the storm **rumbles**, and after the rain comes the **rainbow**. A hands-on lesson
in the main kinds of weather (NMG: observe and describe the weather).

---

## 7. The rainbow 🌈 (colour-cycling block)

The **Regenbogen** block glows and **slowly cycles colour** (red → orange → yellow → green →
blue → indigo → violet → …). Place several in a row or as an **arch** — especially pretty as a
rainbow bridge. A gentle lesson in **colours and sequences** (which colour comes after which?).

---

## 8. Rainbow colours & patterns 🎨

The theme has the **seven rainbow colours** as blocks (red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo,
violet) and four **pattern blocks**: **rainbow**, **cloud**, **sun** and **star**. Use them to
decorate your cloud city, to copy the **pattern tasks** at the boards, or grow them from
**rainbow seeds** (see below).

---

## 9. Planting rainbows 🌱

**Use:** place a **Regenbogen-Samen** (rainbow seed). After a while it **grows** into a random
**rainbow colour block**. Each grown one counts ("Bunte Blöcke: N").

---

## 10. The hot-air balloon 🎈 (flight physics)

**Use:** place a **Heissluftballon** (balloon), then **right-click** to board. **W/S** fly,
**A/D** steer, **jump** = up (warm air rises!), **sneak** = down, **right-click** = get off. A
**second player** can fly along! Why does it fly? **Warm air is lighter** than cold air and
rises — exactly what carries a real hot-air balloon (NMG: explaining phenomena, flight physics).

---

## 11. Altimeter 📏 (up/down)

**Use:** hold the **Höhenmesser** (altimeter) and **left-click** → it tells you **how high** you
are and whether that is **up high** or **down low**. Great for practising **up/down** and
**high/low** (spatial orientation) — especially in the flying balloon.

---

## 12. Explorer camera 📷

**Use:** hold the camera, get near an animal and **left-click** = photo. The nearest
**not-yet-discovered** animal is marked **discovered** in the logbook (range about 12 blocks).
Photograph **all** animals; the logbook shows "Discovered: X/12". Separate from helping.

---

## 13. Sky logbook 📋

**Use:** logbook in hand → **right-click** to open. You see a list of all animals; click one for
the **profile** (name, **zone**, "eats", note), how **often you helped** it, and whether it
has been **discovered**. The top shows your **rank**, the **total helped** and the **discovered
count**.

---

## 14. Ranks & badges 🏆

Every help counts. At certain thresholds your rank goes up (sound + pop-up); from
"Himmels-Forscher" on you also get a **badge item** (right-click it for your tally).

| From … helped | Rank | Badge |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | Himmels-Frischling (sky rookie) | – |
| 5 | Wolkenläufer (cloud walker) | – |
| 10 | Himmels-Forscher (sky researcher) | 🥉 yes |
| 25 | Wolken-Freund (cloud friend) | 🥈 yes |
| 50 | Himmels-Held (sky hero) | 🥇 yes |

Top-left a **HUD** always shows: world · rank · helped.

## 15. Blocks, items & internal names

Everything is generated without external pictures. Internal names (for the creative search or
`/giveme`) begin with `lernwelt_himmelsdorf:`.

**Clouds & sky:** `wolke` · `wolke_glas` (transparent) · `wolkenziegel` · `schwebeland` (island
ground) · `himmelsstein` · `sonnenblock` (glowing) · `sternenblock` (glowing) · `wolkenlampe`
(glowing) · `ballonkorb` (per-face).

**Rainbow colours:** `regenbogen_rot` · `regenbogen_orange` · `regenbogen_gelb` ·
`regenbogen_gruen` · `regenbogen_blau` · `regenbogen_indigo` · `regenbogen_violett`.

**Patterns:** `muster_regenbogen` · `muster_wolke` · `muster_sonne` · `muster_stern`.

**Rainbow:** `regenbogen` (glows & cycles colour; the colour variants appear automatically and
are not in the creative menu).

**Weather & caring:** `wetterstation` (change the weather) · `regenbogen_samen` (grows into a
colour block).

**Letter blocks:** `buchstabe_a` … `buchstabe_z` (A–Z) — for labelling (lay out animal names).
For Umlauts use AE/OE/UE.

> **Own patterns or pictures on blocks?** Yes! A block can use `texture = "..."` instead of
> `color = "#..."` (a pattern without a file, a PNG, or per face `top/side/bottom`). See
> `textures/README.txt`.

**Items:** `logbuch` (sky logbook) · `kamera` · `heissluftballon` (balloon) · `hoehenmesser`
(altimeter) · the mob spawn eggs (e.g. `wolkenschaf`, `sturmvogel`, `regenbogen_pegasus`) ·
badges (`badge_10/25/50`) · the learning boards (`tafel_regenbogenbruecke` / `tafel_wolkenfelder`
/ `tafel_ballonhafen`).

## 17. Settings

In the menu under **Settings → All settings → Mods** (or in `minetest.conf`):

| Setting | Default | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| `lernwelt_show_hud` | on | Progress display top-left |
| `lernwelt_spawn_creatures` | on | Animals appear automatically |
| `lernwelt_big_text` | off | **Larger text** on the learning boards |
| `lernwelt_easy_mode` | off | **Easy mode**: quizzes show only 2 answers |
| `lernwelt_himmelsdorf_starter_kit` | on | Starter kit on first join |

## 18. For parents & teachers 👪

- **Lehrplan 21 (NMG – weather & spatial orientation):** every zone and task is tagged with
  competence areas — **science/NMG** (observing the weather, explaining phenomena / flight
  physics, sky animals), **mathematics** (up/down & position, colour sequences), **German**
  (naming animals & weather) and **art** (the seven rainbow colours).
- **Observing the weather** (sun, clouds, rain, thunderstorm, rainbow) at the weather station is
  the core of the NMG weather topic — tangible and instantly visible.
- **Up/down, high/low** become concrete while **flying** (balloon) and with the **altimeter**
  (spatial orientation).
- `/lernplan` shows all zones and their links at a glance; `/lernfortschritt` shows per child:
  rank, helped, tasks solved, animals met and learning areas covered.
- **Accessibility:** `lernwelt_big_text` (large text) and `lernwelt_easy_mode` (fewer quiz
  options) make it easier to start.

## 19. Little missions & ideas

- 🌈 **Colour row:** lay out the seven rainbow colours in the right order.
- 🐦 **First help:** help 2 rainbow hummingbirds on the bridge.
- ☁️ **Cloud herder:** help 2 cloud sheep in the cloud fields.
- 🌦️ **Weather watcher:** cycle through every weather at the weather station.
- 🎈 **First balloon ride:** fly the balloon high over the cloud city (with two players!).
- 📏 **Height explorer:** measure your highest altitude with the altimeter.
- 🌱 **Rainbow garden:** plant 5 rainbow seeds.
- 📷 **Researcher:** photograph all animals (Discovered 12/12).
- 🌟 **Legend:** find the rare rainbow pegasus.
- 🔤 **Animal sign:** spell an animal name with letter blocks (e.g. SCHWALBE).

## 20. Troubleshooting

- **Animals don't appear automatically:** `lernwelt_spawn_creatures` must be on and a mob API is
  required (VoxeLibre/Mineclonia or `mobs_redo`). You can always place animals with **spawn
  eggs**. Animals need a **solid surface** (cloud platform / island) to appear on.
- **The weather doesn't change:** you must **right-click** the **weather station**. Each click
  advances one weather step.
- **The rainbow doesn't change colour:** freshly placed blocks start by themselves. Use
  `/himmelsdorf_wolkenstadt` for a ready-made glowing rainbow bridge.
- **The balloon sinks:** an **empty** balloon slowly drifts down. Board it (**right-click**) and
  press **jump** to rise.
- **No sound:** sound files are optional. Put your own `.ogg` files in the `sounds/` folder (see
  `sounds/README.txt`) — without them everything works, just silently.

Have fun discovering, naming and protecting! 🌈☁️🎈
