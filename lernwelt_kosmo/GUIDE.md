# Player Guide — Kosmo-Station (Space Station) 🚀🪐

This guide explains **all the elements** of the "Kosmo-Station" learning world,
**how to use them** and **what the tasks and goals are**. Made for **parents and
children playing together** (about 5–7 years old).

> Note: the in-game texts are in **German** (the theme is authored in German), so
> names you see on screen are German; this guide adds English explanations.

> Motto: **Entdecken · Erforschen · Helfen** (Discover · Explore · Help)
>
> There is no "winning" — the point is to discover, help and learn: rescue and
> photograph space friends, solve little tasks, keep the orbit clean and grow a
> galaxy, while practising counting planets, colours, moon gravity and planning a
> mission order (Swiss curriculum *Lehrplan 21*, cycle 1).

---

## 1. Quick start

1. Make a new world with **VoxeLibre** or **Mineclonia** (for the space friends) and
   enable the mods **`lernwelt`** + **`lernwelt_kosmo`**. A flat, calm world is ideal —
   see **[WELT-EINRICHTEN.md](WELT-EINRICHTEN.md)**.
2. Enter the world → you automatically get the **starter kit**.
3. Fast start: type the command **`/kosmo_basis`** — a ready-made dome station with
   boards, a space glider and a moon-gravity strip appears in front of you.

---

## 2. Basic controls

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| **Use / rescue a friend / open a board** | **Right-click** |
| **Photograph / break junk / dig blocks** | **Left-click** (hold) |
| **Build / place an item** | **Right-click** on the ground |
| **Fly (creative)** | Double-jump; then jump = up, sneak = down |

In the child setting, **damage is off** — nothing bad can happen. The sky is a fixed
**starry night** (the station glows from many light blocks), and there are no monsters.

---

## 3. The world: 4 learning zones

| Zone | Friends there | Learning areas |
|---|---|---|
| 🪐 **Bunte Planeten** (colourful planets) | Green alien, Mars-being, Space cat, Plasma bird, Mini-UFO | Maths (counting), science, German |
| ☄️ **Asteroidengürtel** (asteroid belt) | Asteroid crab, Satellite Robi | Maths (counting, sizes), science |
| 🌌 **Sternennebel-Höhle** (dark star nebula cave) | Space jelly, Star-being, Nebula drifter, *Golden comet* | Science (light/dark) |
| 🌑 **Mond-Basis** (moon base) | Mini-robot, Astronaut, Moon hare | Movement (gravity), media/IT, maths |

---

## 4. Learning boards & tasks (the heart of the game)

**Use:** right-click a learning board → a window shows the **activity**, the
**curriculum links** and the zone's **tasks**.

Three task types:

- ❓ **Quiz** — a question with answer buttons. Right = ✓ + reward, wrong = "try again".
- 🎨 **Copy the pattern** — a colour sequence (e.g. blue-yellow-red) is shown at the top.
  Click the colour buttons **in the right order**; a mistake resets it. On the **moon
  base** the pattern is the **rocket launch order** (1 fuel → 2 countdown → 3 launch),
  so you plan a **mission order**.
- 👽 **Rescue goal** — e.g. "rescue 2 green aliens". The progress (x/N) counts up
  automatically as you rescue.

Solved tasks give a sound + praise and count towards your progress
(see `/lernfortschritt`).

---

## 5. Moon gravity 🌑 (the special feature)

Stand on a **gravity block** and feel your movement change:

| Block | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Mond-Boden** (moon ground) | Low gravity — you jump **feather-light and high**. |
| **Sprung-Pad** (jump pad / trampoline) | Bounces you **upward** when you stand on it. |
| **Schwerelos-Feld** (weightless field) | Almost no gravity — you **drift** like in space. |
| **Schwerkraft-Normal** (normal gravity) | Back to **normal** gravity. |

Leave the block and you are automatically normal again — great for practising
moon-hopping (movement / BS) and wondering about the Moon (science / NMG). The blocks
are in the starter kit and creative inventory; `/kosmo_teststation` and `/kosmo_basis`
build a ready-made moon-hop strip.

---

## 6. Rescuing space friends 👽

**Use:** right-click a friend = **rescue** (bring it back to the station). You get
hearts, a sound, a counter entry and (after enough rescues) a new rank.

**All friends:** Green alien (glows), Mars-being (👪 family), Space cat (👪 family),
Plasma bird (glows), Mini-UFO (glows), Asteroid crab, Satellite Robi (glows),
Mini-robot, Astronaut, Moon hare (👪 family), Space jelly (glows), Star-being (glows),
Nebula drifter (glows), and the rare **Golden comet** 🌟.

- 👪 **Families:** rescue a parent (Mars-being, Space cat, Moon hare) and its **young**
  appear, which you can rescue too.
- 🌟 **Golden comet:** does **not** appear by itself. Get it from its **spawn egg**.
- 🛰️ **Living station:** the other friends **appear automatically** on solid ground near
  you (gently capped). Can be turned off (see Settings).

**Spawn eggs:** every friend has an egg. Egg in hand → **right-click** the ground =
place the friend.

---

## 7. Explorer camera 📷

**Use:** hold the camera, get near a friend and **left-click** = photo. The nearest
**not-yet-discovered** friend is marked **discovered** in the logbook (range ~12 blocks).
Photograph **all** friends (logbook shows "Discovered: X/14").

---

## 8. Star logbook 📋

**Use:** logbook in hand → **right-click** to open. You see a list of all friends; click
one for the **profile** (name, home, eats, power), how **often** you rescued it, and
whether it has been **discovered**. The top shows your **rank**, **total rescues** and
**discovered count**.

---

## 9. Ranks & badges 🏆

| From … rescues | Rank | Badge |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | Anwärter (Candidate) | – |
| 5 | Kadett (Cadet) | – |
| 10 | Pilot | 🥉 yes |
| 25 | Commander | 🥈 yes |
| 50 | Sternen-Kapitän (Star captain) | 🥇 yes |

Top-left a **HUD** always shows: world · rank · rescues.

---

## 10. Clean the orbit 🧹 ("Protect")

Sometimes there is **space junk** (metal, old panel, empty tank). **Left-click** to
collect it → the orbit gets cleaner, your counter rises, with milestones:
Stations-Putzer (5) → Orbit-Reiniger (15) → Weltraum-Held (30). Get practice junk with
`/kosmo_schrott` or from the test stations.

---

## 11. Plant stars ⭐ ("Build up")

**Use:** hold a **star seed** → **right-click** the ground = plant. After ~20 s it
**grows** into a colourful planet or star. Each grown star counts towards your **galaxy
counter**. Build your own galaxy piece by piece.

---

## 12. Space glider 🚀

**Use:** place it, **right-click** = get in, then **W/S** fly · **A/D** steer · **jump**
= up · **sneak** = down · **right-click** = get out. Upgrades: glowing thruster, exhaust
trail, and a **second seat** so a **second player** can ride along (parent + child).

---

## 13. Commands

| Command | Effect | Who |
|---|---|---|
| `/lernfortschritt` | Your report: rank, rescues, tasks solved, friends met, learning areas | everyone |
| `/lernplan` | Overview of all zones + Lehrplan-21 links | everyone |
| `/lernwelt` | Status + recommended `minetest.conf` lines | everyone |
| `/kosmo_teststation` | Small ready-made station (boards, glider, moon ground, junk) | admin¹ |
| `/kosmo_basis` | Bigger glass-dome base with a door | admin¹ |
| `/kosmo_schrott` | Scatters junk to clean up | admin¹ |

¹ Needs the `server` privilege — **automatically available in single-player**.

---

## 14. Settings

| Setting | Default | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| `lernwelt_show_hud` | on | Progress display top-left |
| `lernwelt_spawn_creatures` | on | Friends appear automatically on solid ground |
| `lernwelt_big_text` | off | **Larger text** on the learning boards |
| `lernwelt_easy_mode` | off | **Easy mode**: quizzes show only 2 answers |
| `lernwelt_kosmo_starter_kit` | on | Starter kit on first join |

---

## 15. For parents & teachers 👪

- **Lehrplan 21 (cycle 1):** every zone and task is tagged — Maths (counting planets &
  asteroids, patterns, sizes), German (naming), science (celestial bodies: Sun, Moon,
  planets; light/dark), media & informatics (sequences / mission order), movement
  (moon gravity, jumping).
- `/lernplan` shows all zones and links; `/lernfortschritt` shows per child what they
  covered. **Accessibility:** `lernwelt_big_text` and `lernwelt_easy_mode` help starters.

Have fun discovering, exploring and helping! 🚀🪐
