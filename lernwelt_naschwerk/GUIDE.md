# Player Guide — Naschwerk-Tal (Candy Valley) 🍭🍫🐻

This guide explains **all the elements** of the "Naschwerk-Tal" learning world, **how to use
them** and **what the tasks and goals are**. Made for **parents and children playing together**
(about 5–8 years old).

> Note: the in-game texts are in **German** (the theme is authored in German), so names you see
> on screen are German; this guide adds English explanations.

> Motto: **Zählen · Mustern · Naschen** (Count · Pattern · Nibble)
>
> There is no "winning" — the point is to **count, lay patterns and nibble**: find candy
> creatures and match them to their zone, count quantities, lay out colourful **patterns**
> (red–yellow–red …) and name colours, while practising **early maths** (Swiss curriculum
> *Lehrplan 21*, MA.1).

---

## 1. Quick start

1. Make a new world with **VoxeLibre** or **Mineclonia** and enable the mods
   **`lernwelt`** + **`lernwelt_naschwerk`**. (World tips:
   **[WELT-EINRICHTEN.md](WELT-EINRICHTEN.md)**.)
2. Enter the world → you automatically get the **starter kit**.
3. Fast start: type **`/naschwerk_lebkuchenhaus`** — a whole gingerbread house with learning
   boards, a rainbow-lolli wall, a counting table, a candy bowl and a candy cart appears.

---

## 2. Basic controls

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| **Use / help an animal / feed at the bowl / open a board / counting table / board the cart** | **Right-click** |
| **Photograph / dig blocks** | **Left-click** (hold) |
| **Build / place an item (block, cart, sapling …)** | **Right-click** on the ground |
| **Fly (creative)** | Double-jump; then jump = up, sneak = down |

In the child setting, **damage is off** — nothing bad can happen. The time of day is fixed
(bright sunny world), and there are no monsters. All animals are peaceful.

---

## 3. The world: 3 learning zones

Each zone has a **learning board** and matching candy creatures:

| Zone | Animals there | Learning areas |
|---|---|---|
| 🍫 **Schoko-Fluss** (chocolate river) | Chocolate fish, marshmallow swan, cookie turtle, fizzy jellyfish | Counting, colours |
| 🍭 **Lolli-Wald** (lollipop forest) | Cotton-candy sheep, lollipop bird, candy rabbit, caramel squirrel | Patterns, colours, counting |
| 🐻 **Gummi-Hügel** (gummy hills) | Gummy bear, licorice snail, sugar mouse, *rainbow-lolli fairy* | Counting quantities, patterns |

---

## 4. Learning boards & tasks (the learning heart)

**Use:** right-click a learning board → a window shows the **activity**, the **curriculum
links** and the zone's **tasks**.

Three task types:

- ❓ **Quiz** — a question with answer buttons (e.g. "2 and 1 more = ?"). Right = ✓ + reward,
  wrong = "try again".
- 🎨 **Copy the pattern** — a colourful **pattern** is shown at the top (red–yellow–red …). Click
  the colour buttons **in the right order**. A mistake resets it. This practises **patterns and
  colours**.
- 🐾 **Help goal** — e.g. "help 3 gummy bears". The progress (x/N) counts up automatically.

Solved tasks give a sound + praise and count towards your progress (see `/lernfortschritt`).

---

## 5. The counting table (Zähl-Naschtisch) 🔢 (the maths heart)

**Use:** right-click the **Zähl-Naschtisch** → a window shows a random number of **bonbons**
(1–9). Count them and click the **correct number**.

- ✅ **Right:** reward sound + "Richtig gezählt: N". A **new round** starts at once.
- 🤏 **Almost:** a friendly hint ("count again — point at each bonbon").
- ⭐ At 5, 15 and 30 correct answers you get a little award
  (Zähl-Anfänger → Zähl-Profi → Zähl-Meister).

This practises **grasping and counting quantities** directly (MA.1).

---

## 6. Helping candy creatures 🐾

**Use:** right-click an animal = **help**. You get hearts, a sound, a counter entry and (after
enough) a new rank. All animals are **peaceful** — no fighting.

**All candy creatures:**

| Animal (DE / EN) | Zone | "Eats" | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| Schoko-Fisch / Chocolate fish | Chocolate river | Choc sprinkles | 🏊 swims |
| Marshmallow-Schwan / Marshmallow swan | Chocolate river | Sugar pearls | 🏊 swims · 👪 helpers |
| Keks-Schildkröte / Cookie turtle | Chocolate river | Cookie crumbs | 🏊 cookie shell |
| Brause-Qualle / Fizzy jellyfish | Chocolate river | Sherbet | 🏊 💡 glows & fizzes |
| Zuckerwatte-Schaf / Cotton-candy sheep | Lollipop forest | Cotton candy | soft · 👪 helpers |
| Lolli-Vogel / Lollipop bird | Lollipop forest | Bonbons | colourful lolli crest |
| Bonbon-Hase / Candy rabbit | Lollipop forest | Bonbons | nimble · 👪 helpers |
| Karamell-Eichhörnchen / Caramel squirrel | Lollipop forest | Caramel nuts | collects caramel |
| Gummibär / Gummy bear | Gummy hills | Fruit gums | soft & wobbly · 👪 helpers |
| Lakritz-Schnecke / Licorice snail | Gummy hills | Licorice leaves | slow, shell |
| Zucker-Maus / Sugar mouse | Gummy hills | Sugar crumbs | nimble · 👪 helpers |
| **Regenbogen-Lolli-Fee / Rainbow-lolli fairy** | Gummy hills | Rainbow sugar | 🌈 very rare, glows |

- 👪 **Families:** help a "parent" animal (marshmallow swan, cotton-candy sheep, candy rabbit,
  gummy bear, sugar mouse) and small helpers appear, which you can help too.
- 🏊 **Water animals** (chocolate fish, marshmallow swan, cookie turtle, fizzy jellyfish) appear
  **in water** (the chocolate river), the others **on land** near you.
- 🌈 **Rainbow-lolli fairy:** does **not** appear by itself (very rare). Get it from its **spawn
  egg** (in the starter kit / creative inventory).

**Spawn eggs:** every animal has an egg. Egg in hand → **right-click** the ground = place it.

---

## 7. Feeding animals 🍬 (caring)

1. 🧰 Place a **Naschschale** (candy bowl) on the ground.
2. 🍬 Hold a **Bonbon** and **right-click** the bowl → the animals are fed.
3. ⭐ Each feed counts ("Satte Naschtiere: N"). At 5, 15 and 30 you get a little award
   (Naschtier-Freund → Naschtier-Pfleger → Naschtier-Kümmerer).

---

## 8. The rainbow lolli (Regenbogen-Lolli) 🌈

The **Regenbogen-Lolli** is a glowing block that **slowly cycles colour** (red → orange → yellow
→ green → blue → violet → …). Place several side by side to make a glowing **rainbow wall** —
especially pretty in the gingerbread house. A gentle lesson in **colours** (BG.1).

---

## 9. Colourful patterns 🎨

The theme has four **drops blocks** (red, yellow, blue, green) and many more bright candy blocks.
Use them to decorate, to copy the **pattern tasks** at the boards (red–yellow–red …), or grow
them from **lollis** (see below).

---

## 10. Planting lollis 🍭

**Use:** place a **Lolli-Setzling** (lolli sapling). After a while it **grows** into a random
**bright candy block** (drops, lolli, candy cane …). Each grown lolli counts ("Süssigkeits-Blöcke:
N").

---

## 11. The candy cart (Naschwagen) 🛒

**Use:** place a **Naschwagen** (candy cart), then **right-click** to board. **W/S** drive,
**A/D** steer, **jump** = a small hop, **right-click** = get off. A **second player** can ride
along! The cart sprays colourful sprinkles as it rolls — great for exploring the whole valley.

---

## 12. Explorer camera 📷

**Use:** hold the camera, get near an animal and **left-click** = photo. The nearest
**not-yet-discovered** animal is marked **discovered** in the logbook (range about 12 blocks).
Photograph **all** animals; the logbook shows "Discovered: X/12". Separate from helping.

---

## 13. Candy logbook 📋

**Use:** logbook in hand → **right-click** to open. You see a list of all animals; click one for
the **profile** (name, **zone**, "eats", note), how **often you helped** it, and whether it has
been **discovered**. The top shows your **rank**, the **total helped** and the **discovered
count**.

---

## 14. Ranks & badges 🏆

Every help counts. At certain thresholds your rank goes up (sound + pop-up); from "Zucker-Forscher"
on you also get a **badge item** (right-click it for your tally).

| From … helped | Rank | Badge |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | Naschmaus (candy mouse) | – |
| 5 | Bonbon-Sammler (candy collector) | – |
| 10 | Zucker-Forscher (sugar researcher) | 🥉 yes |
| 25 | Naschmeister (candy master) | 🥈 yes |
| 50 | Naschwerk-Held (candy hero) | 🥇 yes |

Top-left a **HUD** always shows: world · rank · helped.

## 15. Blocks, items & internal names

Everything is generated without external pictures. Internal names (for the creative search or
`/giveme`) begin with `lernwelt_naschwerk:`.

**Building & sweets:** `lebkuchen` · `lebkuchen_ziegel` · `zuckerguss` · `schokolade` ·
`weisse_schokolade` · `keks` · `waffel` · `zuckerwatte_rosa` · `zuckerwatte_blau` · `lakritz` ·
`karamell` (warm, glowing) · `naschkiste` (per-face).

**Gummy (translucent):** `gummi_rot` · `gummi_gruen` · `gummi_gelb` · `gummi_orange`.

**Colourful pattern blocks:** `drops_rot` · `drops_gelb` · `drops_blau` · `drops_gruen` ·
`zuckerstange` · `bonbon_streifen` · `lolli_block` · `bonbon_glas` (glowing lamp).

**Rainbow lolli:** `regenbogen_lolli` (glows & cycles colour; the colour variants appear
automatically and are not in the creative menu).

**Counting & caring:** `zaehltisch` (counting game) · `naschschale` (feed with bonbons) ·
`bonbon` (food) · `lolli_setzling` (grows into a candy block).

**Letter blocks:** `buchstabe_a` … `buchstabe_z` (A–Z) — for labelling (lay out candy names).
For Umlauts use AE/OE/UE.

**Items:** `logbuch` (candy logbook) · `kamera` · `naschwagen` (cart) · the mob spawn eggs (e.g.
`gummibaer`, `schoko_fisch`, `regenbogen_fee`) · badges (`badge_10/25/50`) · the learning boards
(`tafel_schoko_fluss` / `tafel_lolli_wald` / `tafel_gummi_huegel`).

> **Own patterns or pictures on blocks?** Yes! A block can use `texture = "..."` instead of
> `color = "#..."` (a pattern without a file, a PNG, or per face `top/side/bottom`). See
> `textures/README.txt`.

## 17. Settings

In the menu under **Settings → All settings → Mods** (or in `minetest.conf`):

| Setting | Default | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| `lernwelt_show_hud` | on | Progress display top-left |
| `lernwelt_spawn_creatures` | on | Animals appear automatically (land & water) |
| `lernwelt_big_text` | off | **Larger text** on the learning boards |
| `lernwelt_easy_mode` | off | **Easy mode**: quizzes show only 2 answers |
| `lernwelt_naschwerk_starter_kit` | on | Starter kit on first join |

## 18. For parents & teachers 👪

- **Lehrplan 21 (focus MA.1 – number & quantity):** every zone and task is tagged with
  competence areas — **mathematics** (grasping & counting quantities, continuing patterns &
  series), **art** (recognising & using colours) and **German** (naming sweets).
- The **counting table** practises **grasping quantities** directly: see → count → match the
  number.
- The **pattern game** at the boards (red–yellow–red …) trains **recognising and continuing
  series**.
- **Feeding** and **helping** make **responsibility & care** tangible.
- `/lernplan` shows all zones and their links at a glance; `/lernfortschritt` shows per child:
  rank, helped, tasks solved, animals met and learning areas covered.
- **Accessibility:** `lernwelt_big_text` (large text) and `lernwelt_easy_mode` (fewer quiz
  options) make it easier to start.

## 19. Little missions & ideas

- 🔢 **Counting hero:** count right 5 times at the counting table.
- 🐻 **Gummy help:** help 3 gummy bears on the gummy hills.
- 🎨 **Pattern artist:** lay out your own red-yellow-red pattern with drops blocks.
- 🌈 **Rainbow wall:** build a row of rainbow lollis and watch them change colour.
- 🍭 **Candy garden:** plant 5 lollis.
- 🍬 **Animal carer:** feed candy creatures until you are "Naschtier-Freund" (5×).
- 🛒 **Candy ride:** ride the cart across the valley (with two players!).
- 📷 **Researcher:** photograph all animals (Discovered 12/12).
- 🌈 **Legend:** find the rare rainbow-lolli fairy.
- 🔤 **Candy sign:** spell a candy word with letter blocks (e.g. LOLLI).

## 20. Troubleshooting

- **Animals don't appear automatically:** `lernwelt_spawn_creatures` must be on and a mob API is
  required (VoxeLibre/Mineclonia or `mobs_redo`). You can always place animals with **spawn eggs**.
- **Water animals missing:** the chocolate fish, marshmallow swan, cookie turtle and fizzy
  jellyfish need **water** nearby (the "chocolate river"). They do not appear on land.
- **Candy bowl does nothing:** you must hold a **bonbon** in your hand (from the starter kit or
  creative inventory).
- **Rainbow lolli doesn't change colour:** freshly placed blocks start by themselves. Use
  `/naschwerk_lebkuchenhaus` for a ready-made glowing rainbow wall.
- **No sound:** sound files are optional. Put your own `.ogg` files in the `sounds/` folder (see
  `sounds/README.txt`) — without them everything works, just silently.

Have fun counting, patterning and nibbling! 🍭🍫🐻
