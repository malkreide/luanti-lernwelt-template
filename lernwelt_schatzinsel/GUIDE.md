# Player Guide — Schatzinsel (Treasure Island) 🏴‍☠️🗺️

This guide explains **all the elements** of the "Schatzinsel" learning world,
**how to use them** and **what the tasks and goals are**. Made for **parents and
children playing together** (about 5–7 years old).

> Note: the in-game texts are in **German** (the theme is authored in German), so
> names you see on screen are German; this guide adds English explanations. German
> names appear with an English gloss where helpful.

> Motto: **Karte lesen · Schätze zählen · Insel entdecken**
> (Read the map · Count the treasure · Explore the island)
>
> There is no "winning" — the point is to discover, orient yourself and count:
> follow a **treasure map** and a **compass**, **count gold coins**, rescue and
> photograph animals, solve little tasks and complete a **treasure hunt**, while
> practising spatial orientation and counting (Swiss curriculum *Lehrplan 21*,
> cycle 1: NMG.3 / MA.3 and MA.1).

> 🏴‍☠️ **Friendly pirates:** no fighting, no monsters, no scary skulls. The island
> flag shows a **parrot**.

---

## 1. Quick start

1. Create a world with an **island and water** — see **[WELT-EINRICHTEN.md](WELT-EINRICHTEN.md)**.
2. Make a new world with **VoxeLibre** or **Mineclonia** and enable the mods
   **`lernwelt`** + **`lernwelt_schatzinsel`**.
3. Enter the world → you automatically get the **starter kit**. Fast start: type the
   command **`/schatzinsel_schiff`** — a ready-made treasure ship with a dock, learning
   boards and a raft appears in front of you.

---

## 2. Basic controls

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| **Use / rescue an animal / open a board or signpost** | **Right-click** |
| **Photograph / collect a gold coin / dig blocks** | **Left-click** (hold) |
| **Build / place an item** | **Right-click** on the ground |
| **Climb** | Walk up against a **rope ladder** (Strickleiter) |
| **Fly (creative)** | Double-jump; then jump = up, sneak = down |

In the child setting, **damage and drowning are off** — nothing bad can happen. The
time of day is fixed (no scary night), and there are no monsters.

---

## 3. The world: 3 learning zones

Each zone is a "place" with a **learning board** and matching animals:

| Zone | Animals there | Learning areas |
|---|---|---|
| 🏝️ **Insel** (island) | Parrot, Monkey, Beach crab, Land turtle, Seagull, *Golden parrot* | NMG.3, MA.3, MA.1 |
| 🕳️ **Tropfsteinhöhle** (dripstone cave) | Bat, Glow beetle, Cave cricket, Crystal snail | NMG.3, MA.1, NMG.2 |
| 🐠 **Lagune** (lagoon) | Colourful fish, Starfish, Sea turtle, Octopus | NMG.2, MA.1, MA.3 |

---

## 4. Learning boards & tasks (the heart of the game)

**Use:** right-click a learning board → a window shows the **activity**, the
**curriculum links** and the zone's **tasks**.

Three task types:

- ❓ **Quiz** — a question with answer buttons (e.g. **counting**, or naming a
  **compass direction**). Right = ✓ + reward, wrong = "try again".
- 🎨 **Copy the pattern** — a colour pattern is shown; click the colour buttons in the
  right order. A mistake resets the pattern.
- 🐠 **Rescue goal** — e.g. "rescue 2 parrots". The progress (x/N) counts up
  automatically as you rescue.

Solved tasks give a sound + praise and count towards your progress
(see `/lernfortschritt`).

**Which tasks are there?**

| Zone | Tasks |
|---|---|
| Island | Quiz **"how many gold coins?"** (counting) · Quiz **"which way does the compass needle point?"** · Rescue 2 parrots |
| Dripstone cave | Quiz **"does a dripstone point up or down?"** · Quiz **"how many coins?"** (counting) · Rescue 2 bats · **pattern** crystal-gold-crystal |
| Lagoon | Quiz "who swims?" · Quiz **"how many fish?"** (counting) · Rescue 2 fish |

---

## 5. Treasure compass 🧭 (orientation)

**Use:** hold the compass → **left-click**. It tells you:

- **which way you face** — Norden/Osten/Süden/Westen (N/E/S/W),
- **where you are** — down on the beach / on deck, a few blocks up the slope,
  **up** in the lookout (Mastkorb), or **deep down** in the dripstone cave,
- **how many gold coins** you have counted so far.

A hands-on way to practise compass directions and **up/down**.

---

## 6. Treasure map 🗺️ (reading the map)

**Use:** hold the treasure map → **left-click**. It "reads" the direction to the
nearest **signpost** of the treasure hunt for you (with a rough distance). Follow the
direction — just like reading a real map.

Tip: `/schatzinsel_schnitzeljagd` lays out a treasure hunt, then the map shows something.

---

## 7. Signposts 🪧 (picture hints / Wegweiser)

Signposts are little wooden boards showing a **picture**: island, palm, wave, chest,
shell, anchor, **gold coin**, or a **direction arrow** (N/E/S/W, up/down). Children
"read" the picture as a hint.

**Use:**
- **Right-click** = next picture (cycle through).
- **Sneak + right-click** = a chooser to pick any picture directly.

That's how you build your **own treasure hunt**: place signposts with arrows pointing
from one to the next — ending at a treasure chest.

> Signposts placed by the **automatic treasure hunt** (below) instead show a **text
> hint** with a direction when right-clicked.

---

## 8. Treasure hunt 🧩 (Schnitzeljagd)

**Use:** type **`/schatzinsel_schnitzeljagd`**. A **course** appears: several signposts
on small posts, each showing a picture + an arrow **to the next** — the last leads to a
**treasure chest**.

Follow the arrows (N/E/S/…, one goes **up**!) from sign to sign until you find the
treasure. **Right-click** the chest = open it: sound, sparkle, a counter rises and some
**gold** goes into your inventory.

| Treasures opened | Title |
|---|---|
| 1 | Schatzsucher (Treasure seeker) |
| 3 | Karten-Leser (Map reader) |
| 5 | Schatzmeister (Treasure master) |

---

## 9. Counting gold coins 🪙 (MA.1)

The **heart of counting**: gold coins lie in the sand and the cave. **Left-click** to
collect one → a personal **counter** rises by one, with a sound and a message
("you have now counted 7 gold coins"). You count along, one by one.

| Coins counted | Title |
|---|---|
| 5 | Münzen-Zähler (Coin counter) |
| 15 | Schatz-Buchhalter (Treasure accountant) |
| 30 | Goldsammler (Gold collector) |

Get practice coins with `/schatzinsel_muenzen` or from the test station. Your coin
count also shows in the **treasure compass**.

> 🔟 You can take the **number blocks 0–9** (`zahl_0` … `zahl_9`) and build the number
> of your coins as a big numeral in the world.

---

## 10. Rescuing animals 🐠

**Use:** right-click an animal = **rescue**. You get hearts, a sound, a counter entry
and (after enough rescues) a new rank.

**All animals:**

| Animal (DE / EN) | Home | Eats | Power | Note |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Papagei / Parrot | Island | Fruit | Mimics everything | 👪 family |
| Äffchen / Monkey | Island | Bananas | Climbs fast | 👪 family |
| Strand-Krabbe / Beach crab | Island | Algae | Walks sideways | |
| Land-Schildkröte / Land turtle | Island | Beach grass | Carries its house | 👪 family |
| Möwe / Seagull | Island | Fish | Glides in the wind | |
| Fledermaus / Bat | Cave | Moths | Hears in the dark | |
| Leuchtkäfer / Glow beetle | Cave | Moss | Glows | 💡 glows |
| Höhlen-Grille / Cave cricket | Cave | Mushrooms | Jumps far | |
| Kristall-Schnecke / Crystal snail | Cave | Drip water | Glitters | 💡 glows |
| Bunter Fisch / Colourful fish | Lagoon | Algae | Swims fast | 🌊 swims |
| Seestern / Starfish | Lagoon | Mussels | Arms grow back | 🌊 swims |
| Wasserschildkröte / Sea turtle | Lagoon | Sea grass | Paddles with flippers | 🌊 swims, 👪 family |
| Tintenfisch / Octopus | Lagoon | Crabs | Squirts ink | 🌊 swims |
| **Goldener Papagei / Golden parrot** | Island | Sunlight | Rare & shiny | 🌟 very rare, glows |

- 👪 **Families:** rescue a parent (parrot, monkey, turtles) and its **babies** appear.
- 🌟 **Golden parrot:** does **not** appear by itself (very rare). Get it from its
  **spawn egg** (in the starter kit / creative inventory).
- 🌊 **Living island:** the other animals **appear automatically** near you, on land and
  in water (gently capped). Can be turned off (see Settings).

**Spawn eggs:** every animal has an egg. Egg in hand → **right-click** the ground/water.

---

## 11. Explorer camera 📷

**Use:** hold the camera, get near an animal and **left-click** = photo. The nearest
**not-yet-discovered** animal is marked **discovered** in the logbook (range ~12 blocks).
Photograph all **14** animals.

---

## 12. Logbook (Bordbuch) 📋

**Use:** logbook in hand → **right-click** to open. You see all animals; click one for
the **profile** (name, home, eats, power), how **often you rescued** it and whether it
has been **discovered**. The top shows your **rank**, total rescues and discovered count.

Top-left a **HUD** always shows: world · rank · rescues.

---

## 14. Raft ⛵ (Floss)

**Use:**
1. **Place** the raft item (right-click on the ground / into water).
2. **Right-click** the raft = **board**.
3. Sail: **W/S** forward/back · **A/D** steer · **jump** = up · **sneak** = down ·
   **right-click** = get off.
- 👨‍👧 **Second seat** — a **second player** can **ride along** with right-click.

## 17. For parents & teachers 👪

- **Lehrplan 21 (cycle 1):** every zone and task is tagged with competence areas —
  **Maths**: counting (MA.1, gold coins), spatial orientation (MA.3, compass directions,
  up/down); **science (NMG)**: exploring and orienting in spaces (NMG.3, map/compass),
  discovering animals in their habitat (NMG.2).
- `/lernplan` shows all zones and their links at a glance.
- `/lernfortschritt` shows per child: rank, rescues, tasks solved, animals met and the
  learning areas covered.
- **Accessibility:** `lernwelt_big_text` (large text) and `lernwelt_easy_mode` (fewer
  quiz options) make it easier to start.

## 18. Little missions & ideas

- 🟢 **First steps:** rescue 5 animals → rank "Maat".
- 🪙 **Counting:** collect & count 15 gold coins → "Schatz-Buchhalter".
- 🧭 **Navigator:** have the compass name all four directions.
- 🧩 **Treasure seeker:** complete a whole treasure hunt to the chest.
- 🧗 **Lookout:** climb the rope ladder up to the lookout (oben!) and read the signpost.
- 📷 **Researcher:** photograph all 14 animals.
- 🌟 **Legend:** rescue the rare golden parrot.
- 👨‍👧 **Team trip:** ride the raft together across the lagoon.

---

## 19. Troubleshooting

- **Lagoon animals flop on land / disappear:** they need **water**. Go to the water or
  build a pool (see WELT-EINRICHTEN.md).
- **No sound:** sound files are optional. Put your own `.ogg` files in the `sounds/`
  folder — without them everything works, just silently.
- **The treasure map shows nothing:** a **treasure hunt** must be nearby — start one with
  `/schatzinsel_schnitzeljagd`.
- **Animals don't appear automatically:** `lernwelt_spawn_creatures` must be on and a mob
  API is required (VoxeLibre/Mineclonia or `mobs_redo`). You can always place animals with
  **spawn eggs**.

Have fun reading maps, counting and exploring! 🏴‍☠️🗺️🪙
