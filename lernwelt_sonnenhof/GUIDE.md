# Player Guide — Sonnenhof (Magic Farm) 🌻🐄🍞

This guide explains **all the elements** of the "Sonnenhof" learning world, **how to use them**
and **what the tasks and goals are**. Made for **parents and children playing together**
(about 5–8 years old).

> Note: the in-game texts are in **German** (the theme is authored in German), so names you see
> on screen are German; this guide adds English explanations.

> Motto: **Säen · Pflegen · Ernten** (Sow · Care · Harvest)
>
> There is no "winning" — the point is to **sow, care and harvest**: turn a seed into bread,
> care for and count the farm animals and understand their food, while learning about **plants
> and animals** (Swiss curriculum *Lehrplan 21*, NMG – nature, people, society).

---

## 1. Quick start

1. Make a new world with **VoxeLibre** or **Mineclonia** and enable the mods
   **`lernwelt`** + **`lernwelt_sonnenhof`** (for real animals & plants also add **`farming`**
   + **`animalia`**). World tips: **[WELT-EINRICHTEN.md](WELT-EINRICHTEN.md)**.
2. Enter the world → you automatically get the **starter kit**.
3. Fast start: type **`/sonnenhof_scheune`** — a whole farm with a barn, learning boards, a
   wheat field, an animal pen, the magic mill and a feeding trough appears.

---

## 2. Basic controls

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| **Use / help an animal / feed at the trough / open a board / grind at the mill** | **Right-click** |
| **Water a field** (watering can in hand) | **Right-click** the field |
| **Photograph / dig blocks / harvest** | **Left-click** (hold) |
| **Build / place an item (block, seed, spawn egg …)** | **Right-click** on the ground |
| **Fly (creative)** | Double-jump; then jump = up, sneak = down |

In the child setting, **damage is off** — nothing bad can happen. The time of day is fixed
(bright sunny world), and there are no monsters. All animals are peaceful.

## 3. The world: 3 learning zones

| Zone (DE) | Animals there | Learning areas |
|---|---|---|
| 🌾 **Felder** (fields) | Bee, field mouse, stork, farm cat, farm dog | Plants, seed-to-bread, patience |
| 🐄 **Tiergehege** (animal pen) | Chicken, cow, sheep, pig, goat, duck, donkey, *golden lucky hen* | Animal care, counting, food chain |
| 🏭 **Mühle** (mill) | (field mice nibble the grain) | Grain-to-bread, sequence |

## 4. From seed to bread 🌾🍞 (the heart)

The farm makes the whole chain **tangible**, in five steps:

1. 🌱 **Sow:** hold **Weizen-Samen** (wheat seed) and **right-click** the ground (best on
   **Ackererde**, tilled soil). A little wheat stalk appears.
2. 💧 **Water:** hold the **Zauber-Giesskanne** (magic watering can) and **right-click** the
   field. Every plant nearby **grows one stage**. (They also grow on their own — only slowly.
   That teaches **patience**!)
3. 🌾 **Harvest:** when the wheat is **ripe** (golden), **dig it** (left-click). You get
   **Korn** (grain) and a new seed.
4. 🏭 **Grind:** hold **Korn** and **right-click** the **Zaubermühle** (magic mill) → you get
   **Mehl** (flour).
5. 🍞 **Bake:** hold **Mehl** and **right-click** the mill → you get **Brot** (bread)!

> 🌟 **With Farming Redo:** if the `farming` (Farming Redo) mod is installed, you can sow and
> harvest **real** crops — the **watering can** grows real Farming-Redo plants too, and the
> **magic mill** grinds real wheat to flour and bakes bread. The theme plays directly with that
> mod.

---

## 5. Magic watering can 💧 (patience & care)

**Use:** watering can in hand, **right-click** a field. Every **growing plant** nearby jumps
**one growth stage** (the theme's own wheat **and** real Farming-Redo plants). Blue water drops
sprinkle down.

- ⭐ Each watering that grows something counts. At 5, 15 and 30 you get a little award
  (Giess-Anfänger → Giess-Profi → Giess-Meister).
- 🌱 Nothing on the field yet? The game tells you to "sow seeds first".

The lesson: **plants need water, sun and time** (NMG / patience).

---

## 6. The magic mill 🏭 (grain → flour → bread)

**Use:** **right-click** the **Zaubermühle** while holding the right item:

- 🌾 **Korn** (grain) → you get **Mehl** (flour).
- ⚪ **Mehl** (flour) → you get **Brot** (bread).

Each step counts (Müllerlehrling → Müller → Müllermeister). With **Farming Redo** the mill also
grinds real `farming:wheat` to `farming:flour` and bakes `farming:bread`.

---

## 7. The animal pen & animal care 🐄🍽️

1. 🧰 Place a **Futtertrog** (feeding trough) — or use the one in the pen.
2. 🌾 Hold **Tierfutter** (animal feed) or **Korn** and **right-click** the trough → **every
   animal nearby** gets a little heart.
3. ⭐ Each feed counts. At 5, 15 and 30 you get a little award
   (Tierfreund → Tierpfleger → Hof-Tierpfleger).

This works for the theme's own animals **and** for real **Animalia** animals when that mod is
installed.

## 8. Helping farm animals 🐾

**Use:** right-click an animal = **help**. You get hearts, a sound, a counter entry and (after
enough) a new rank. All animals are **peaceful** — no fighting.

| Animal (DE / EN) | Zone | "Eats" | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| Huhn / Chicken | Pen | Grain | lays eggs · 👪 helpers |
| Kuh / Cow | Pen | Grass & hay | gives milk · 👪 helpers |
| Schaf / Sheep | Pen | Grass | gives wool · 👪 helpers |
| Schwein / Pig | Pen | Turnip peel | wallows · 👪 helpers |
| Ziege / Goat | Pen | Leaves & grass | climbs |
| Ente / Duck | Pen | Water plants | 🏊 swims · 👪 helpers |
| Esel / Donkey | Pen | Oats & hay | carries sacks |
| Hofkatze / Farm cat | Fields | Milk | catches mice |
| Hofhund / Farm dog | Fields | Bones | guards the farm |
| Biene / Bee | Fields | Nectar | 💡 pollinates the blossoms |
| Feldmaus / Field mouse | Fields | Grain | nimble · 👪 helpers |
| Storch / Stork | Fields | Frogs | stalks the meadow |
| **Goldenes Glückshuhn / Golden lucky hen** | Pen | Gold grain | 🌟 very rare, glows |

- 👪 **Families:** help a "parent" animal (chicken, cow, sheep, pig, duck, field mouse) and
  small helpers appear, which you can help too.
- 🏊 **The duck** appears **in water** (a farm pond); the others **on land** near you.
- 🌟 **Golden lucky hen:** does **not** appear by itself (very rare). Get it from its **spawn
  egg** (in the starter kit / creative inventory).

**Spawn eggs:** every animal has an egg. Egg in hand → **right-click** the ground = place it.
With **Animalia** real animal eggs are added to the starter kit too.

## 9. Learning boards & tasks

**Use:** right-click a learning board → a window shows the **activity**, the **curriculum
links** and the zone's **tasks**.

Three task types:

- ❓ **Quiz** — a question with answer buttons (e.g. "What does the chicken give?"). Right = ✓ +
  reward, wrong = "try again".
- 🎨 **Copy the seed row** — a **pattern** is shown at the top (green–yellow–green …). Click the
  colour buttons **in the right order**. This practises laying tidy rows.
- 🐾 **Help goal** — e.g. "help 3 chickens". The progress (x/N) counts up automatically.

| Zone | Tasks |
|---|---|
| Felder | Quiz "What does a seed need?" · **pattern** seed row green-yellow-green · help 3 bees |
| Tiergehege | Quiz "What does the chicken give?" · Quiz "What does the cow eat?" · help 3 chickens |
| Mühle | Quiz "What does the mill make from grain?" · Quiz "What comes last?" · help 2 field mice |

Solved tasks give a sound + praise and count towards your progress (see `/lernfortschritt`).

## 10. Sunflowers & farm décor 🌻

Place **Sonnenblumen** (sunflowers — the "sun" in Sonnenhof) and the glowing **Hoflaterne**
(barn lantern) for a cosy mood. Build your own farm with **straw bales**, **timber frame**,
**barn boards** and **pasture fence**.

---

## 11. Explorer camera 📷

**Use:** hold the camera, get near an animal and **left-click** = photo. The nearest
**not-yet-discovered** animal is marked **discovered** in the logbook (range about 12 blocks).
Photograph **all** animals; the logbook shows "Discovered: X/13". Separate from helping.

---

## 12. Sonnenhof logbook 📋

**Use:** logbook in hand → **right-click** to open. You see a list of all animals; click one for
the **profile** (name, **zone**, "eats", note), how **often you helped** it, and whether it has
been **discovered**. The top shows your **rank**, the **total helped** and the **discovered
count**.

---

## 13. Ranks & badges 🏆

Every help counts. At certain thresholds your rank goes up (sound + pop-up); from "Hofhelfer" on
you also get a **badge item** (right-click it for your tally).

| From … helped | Rank | Badge |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | Hofkind (farm child) | – |
| 5 | Jungbauer (young farmer) | – |
| 10 | Hofhelfer (farm helper) | 🥉 yes |
| 25 | Bauernmeister (master farmer) | 🥈 yes |
| 50 | Sonnenhof-Held (farm hero) | 🥇 yes |

Top-left a **HUD** always shows: world · rank · helped.

## 14. Blocks, items & internal names

Everything is generated without external pictures. Internal names (for the creative search or
`/giveme`) begin with `lernwelt_sonnenhof:`.

**Building & farm:** `scheunenwand` · `fachwerk` · `scheunendach` · `holzbalken` · `strohballen`
· `stroh` · `ackererde` · `blumenwiese` · `feldweg` · `weidezaun` · `mehlsack` · `hoflaterne`
(glowing).

**Stations:** `muehle` (magic mill: grain → flour → bread) · `futtertrog` (animal care).

**Plants:** `weizen_samen` (sow) · `weizen_1` … `weizen_4` (grows; ripe = harvest) ·
`sonnenblume` (décor).

**Food & feed:** `korn` (grain) · `mehl` (flour) · `brot` (bread) · `tierfutter` (animal feed).

**Items:** `logbuch` (logbook) · `kamera` (camera) · `giesskanne` (watering can) · the mob spawn
eggs (e.g. `huhn`, `kuh`, `goldhuhn`) · badges (`badge_10/25/50`) · the learning boards
(`tafel_felder` / `tafel_tiergehege` / `tafel_muehle`).

> **With Farming Redo / Animalia** their own items are added too (e.g. `farming:seed_wheat`,
> `farming:flour`, `farming:bread`, `animalia:chicken` …). The theme uses them directly.

> **Own patterns or pictures on blocks?** Yes! A block can use `texture = "..."` instead of
> `color = "#..."` (a pattern without a file, a PNG, or per face `top/side/bottom`). See
> `textures/README.txt`.

## 17. For parents & teachers 👪

- **Lehrplan 21 (focus NMG – nature, people, society):** every zone and task is tagged —
  **NMG** (explore & care for animals and plants; from seed to bread / origin of food), plus
  **mathematics** (counting animals & harvest), **German** (naming farm animals & plants) and
  **cross-curricular** (patience, responsibility, care).
- **From seed to bread** makes the **origin of our food** tangible: sow → water → harvest →
  grind → bake.
- The **watering can** shows that plants need **water, sun and time** (patience).
- **Caring** at the trough and **helping** make **responsibility & care** tangible.
- The **food chain** is discussed in the pen (what does the cow eat? what does it give us?).
- `/lernplan` shows all zones and links at a glance; `/lernfortschritt` shows per child: rank,
  helped, tasks solved, animals met and learning areas covered.
- **Accessibility:** `lernwelt_big_text` (large text) and `lernwelt_easy_mode` (fewer quiz
  options) make it easier to start.

## 18. Little missions & ideas

- 🌱 **Harvest helper:** sow a seed, water it and harvest your first grain.
- 🍞 **Baker:** turn grain into flour and flour into bread.
- 🐔 **Chicken help:** help 3 chickens in the pen.
- 🍽️ **Animal carer:** care for animals at the trough until you are "Tierfreund" (5×).
- 🐝 **Bee friend:** help the bees on the fields.
- 🌻 **Sun garden:** plant a row of sunflowers in front of the barn.
- 🎨 **Row artist:** lay the seed-row pattern at the fields board.
- 📷 **Researcher:** photograph all animals (Discovered 13/13).
- 🌟 **Legend:** find the rare golden lucky hen.

## 19. Troubleshooting

- **Animals don't appear automatically:** `lernwelt_spawn_creatures` must be on and a mob API is
  required (VoxeLibre/Mineclonia or `mobs_redo`). You can always place animals with **spawn eggs**.
- **The duck is missing:** it needs **water** nearby (a farm pond). It does not appear on land.
- **The mill does nothing:** you must hold **Korn** (grain) or **Mehl** (flour). Grain comes from
  **harvesting ripe (golden) wheat** or the starter kit.
- **The trough does nothing:** you must hold **Tierfutter** (feed) or **Korn**.
- **The wheat doesn't grow:** freshly sown plants grow **slowly by themselves** — or **water**
  them with the watering can to speed it up.
- **No sound:** sound files are optional. Put your own `.ogg` files in the `sounds/` folder (see
  `sounds/README.txt`) — without them everything works, just silently.
