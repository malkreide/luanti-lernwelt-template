# Player Guide — Summ-Garten (Bees & Flowers Garden) 🐝🌸🍯

This guide explains **all the elements** of the "Summ-Garten" learning world, **how to use them**
and **what the tasks and goals are**. Made for **parents and children playing together**
(about 5–8 years old).

> Note: the in-game texts are in **German** (the theme is authored in German), so names you see
> on screen are German; this guide adds English explanations.

> Motto: **Bestäuben · Zählen · Muster legen** (Pollinate · Count · Lay patterns)
>
> There is no "winning" — the point is to **explore**: how do bees carry pollen from blossom to
> blossom (pollination)? How does a blossom become a fruit? Why is a honeycomb cell a **hexagon**?
> The garden connects **nature (NMG)** with **geometry and counting (mathematics)**.

---

## 1. Quick start

1. Make a new world with **VoxeLibre** or **Mineclonia** and enable the mods
   **`lernwelt`** + **`lernwelt_summgarten`**. World tips:
   **[WELT-EINRICHTEN.md](WELT-EINRICHTEN.md)**.
2. Enter the world → you automatically get the **starter kit**.
3. Fast start: type **`/summgarten_bienenstock`** — a whole garden with a beehive, learning
   boards, a flower meadow, an orchard tree with blossoms, a wall of honeycombs and the honey
   extractor appears.

## 2. Basic controls

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| **Use / help an animal / open a board / extract honey / count honey** | **Right-click** |
| **Pollinate** (brush in hand) | **Right-click** a flower/blossom |
| **Photograph / dig blocks / harvest** | **Left-click** (hold) |
| **Build / place an item (block, flower, spawn egg …)** | **Right-click** on the ground |
| **Fly (creative)** | Double-jump; then jump = up, sneak = down |

In the child setting, **damage is off** — nothing bad can happen. The time of day is fixed
(bright sunny world), and there are no monsters. All animals are peaceful.

## 3. The world: 3 learning zones

| Zone (DE) | Animals there | Learning areas |

| 🍎 **Obstgarten** (orchard) | Ladybug, caterpillar, earthworm, ant, hedgehog, blackbird | Blossom to fruit, counting |

## 4. Pollination 🐝🌸 (the heart, NMG.1)

Bees carry **pollen** from blossom to blossom — that is **pollination**. In the Summ-Garten you do
it yourself with the **Bestäubungs-Pinsel** (pollen brush):

1. 🖌️ Hold the **brush** and **right-click** a **flower**. Yellow **pollen** puffs up — and you
   **collect Nektar** (nectar).
2. 🌸 **Right-click** an **Obstblüte** (fruit blossom): it gets pollinated and a small **fruit**
   starts to grow.
3. 🍎 The fruit grows through a few stages until it is **ripe** (red). **Dig it** (left-click) →
   you get an **Apfel** (apple) and a new blossom.

> 🔑 **Important:** **No pollination, no fruit.** A blossom stays a blossom until you brush it.
> This makes the importance of bees tangible.

---

## 5. From nectar to honey 🍯 (honey extractor)

**Use:** **right-click** the **Honigschleuder** (honey extractor) while holding **Nektar** →
you get **Honig** (honey).

- 🌸 You get nectar by **pollinating flowers** with the brush.
- ⭐ Each step counts (Imker-Lehrling → Imker → Imkermeister — beekeeper ranks).

---

## 6. Counting honey drops 🔢 (MA.1)

In the honey store you find **filled honeycombs** (they glow golden).

1. 🍯 **Right-click** a **filled honeycomb** → you harvest **one drop of honey**.
2. 🔢 The game **counts** along ("Honigtropfen gezählt: N"). At 5, 15 and 30 you get a little
   award (Honig-Sammler → Honig-Kenner → Honig-Meister).
3. ♻️ The cell goes **empty** and **refills after a short while** — so you can keep counting.

Counting becomes tangible: one drop, two drops, three … (maths – grasping quantities).

---

## 7. Hexagon patterns: the honeycombs 📐 (MA.2 – form & space)

The **mathematical heart**: a honeycomb cell is a **hexagon** (6 corners, 6 sides). Bees build
their combs in a regular **hexagon pattern**.

- 🧱 With the **honeycomb blocks** (yellow & orange) you build your own **patterns** (e.g.
  yellow-orange-yellow-orange …).
- 🗒️ At the honey-store board you **continue the honeycomb pattern** (pattern task) and answer
  "**How many corners has a hexagon?**" (answer: **6**).

So the garden links **nature** (bees build combs) with **geometry** (hexagons, patterns).

---

## 8. Colourful flowers 🎨 (colours, BG)

There are **six flowers** in six colours: **red, yellow, blue, purple, white, orange**. Place them
on the meadow, **name the colours** and lay colourful **rows** (colour patterns). The meadow board
has the pattern task **red–yellow–blue**.

## 9. Helping garden animals 🐾

**Use:** right-click an animal = **help**. You get hearts, a sound, a counter entry and (after
enough) a new rank. All animals are **peaceful** — no fighting.

| Animal (DE / EN) | Zone | "Eats" | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| Biene / Bee | Meadow | Nectar | 💡 pollinates · 👪 helpers |
| Hummel / Bumblebee | Meadow | Nectar & pollen | chubby pollinator · 👪 helpers |
| Schmetterling / Butterfly | Meadow | Nectar | colourful, pollinates in flight |
| Libelle / Dragonfly | Meadow | Small insects | shimmers over the pond |
| Grashüpfer / Grasshopper | Meadow | Grass | jumps far |
| Marienkäfer / Ladybug | Orchard | Aphids | eats pests · 👪 helpers |
| Raupe / Caterpillar | Orchard | Leaves | becomes a butterfly |
| Regenwurm / Earthworm | Orchard | Soil & leaves | loosens the soil |
| Ameise / Ant | Orchard | Seeds & crumbs | works in a team · 👪 helpers |
| Igel / Hedgehog | Orchard | Snails & worms | rolls into a ball |
| Amsel / Blackbird | Orchard | Worms & berries | sings in the morning |
| Bienenkönigin / Queen bee | Honey store | Royal jelly | 💡 heart of the colony |
| **Goldene Honigbiene / Golden honey bee** | Honey store | Gold nectar | 🌟 very rare, glows |

- 👪 **Families:** help a "parent" animal (bee, bumblebee, ladybug, ant) and small helpers appear,
  which you can help too.
- 🌟 **Golden honey bee:** does **not** appear by itself (very rare). Get it from its **spawn
  egg** (in the starter kit / creative inventory).

**Spawn eggs:** every animal has an egg. Egg in hand → **right-click** the ground = place it.

## 10. Learning boards & tasks

**Use:** right-click a learning board → a window shows the **activity**, the **curriculum links**
and the zone's **tasks**.

Three task types:

- ❓ **Quiz** — a question with answer buttons. Right = ✓ + reward, wrong = "try again".
- 🎨 **Copy the pattern** — a **pattern** is shown at the top (colours). Click the colour buttons
  **in the right order**.
- 🐾 **Help goal** — e.g. "help 3 bees". The progress (x/N) counts up automatically.

| Zone | Tasks |
|---|---|
| Blumenwiese | Quiz "What do bees do at the blossoms?" · **pattern** red-yellow-blue · help 3 bees |
| Obstgarten | Quiz "A pollinated blossom becomes …?" · Quiz "How many legs has a bee?" (counting) · help 3 ladybugs |
| Honiglager | **pattern** honeycomb yellow-orange-yellow-orange (hexagon) · Quiz "How many corners has a hexagon?" · help 2 bumblebees |

Solved tasks give a sound + praise and count towards your progress (see `/lernfortschritt`).

---

## 11. Explorer camera 📷

**Use:** hold the camera, get near an animal and **left-click** = photo. The nearest
**not-yet-discovered** animal is marked **discovered** in the logbook (range about 12 blocks).
Photograph **all** animals; the logbook shows "Discovered: X/13". Separate from helping.

---

## 12. Summ-Garten logbook 📋

**Use:** logbook in hand → **right-click** to open. You see a list of all animals; click one for
the **profile** (name, **zone**, "eats", note), how **often you helped** it, and whether it has
been **discovered**. The top shows your **rank**, the **total helped** and the **discovered
count**.

---

## 13. Ranks & badges 🏆

Every help counts. At certain thresholds your rank goes up (sound + pop-up); from "Bienenhelfer"
on you also get a **badge item** (right-click it for your tally).

| From … helped | Rank | Badge |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | Gartenkind (garden child) | – |
| 5 | Blütenfreund (blossom friend) | – |
| 10 | Bienenhelfer (bee helper) | 🥉 yes |
| 25 | Gartenmeister (garden master) | 🥈 yes |
| 50 | Summ-Garten-Held (garden hero) | 🥇 yes |

Top-left a **HUD** always shows: world · rank · helped.

**Plants:** `blume_rot` / `_gelb` / `_blau` / `_lila` / `_weiss` / `_orange` (colours) ·

**Food & collecting:** `nektar` (nectar) · `honig` (honey) · `apfel` (apple) · `wachs` (beeswax).

## 17. For parents & teachers 👪

- **Lehrplan 21 — NMG meets mathematics:** every zone and task is tagged — **NMG.1** (exploring
  nature: pollination, habitats), **NMG.2** (naming animals & plants), **MA.2** (form & space:
  patterns and shapes — the **hexagon** of the comb), **MA.1** (grasping quantities and
  **counting**) and **BG.1** (perceiving & naming colours).
- **Pollination** makes a central natural link tangible: bees carry pollen → the blossom becomes
  a fruit. The brush shows: no pollination, no fruit.
- **Hexagons/honeycombs** connect nature and **geometry**: a comb cell is a hexagon; many cells
  form a regular **pattern** that children recognise and continue.
- **Counting** becomes concrete with the **honey drops** (grasping quantities, naming numbers).
- `/lernplan` shows all zones and links at a glance; `/lernfortschritt` shows per child: rank,
  helped, tasks solved, animals met and learning areas covered.
- **Accessibility:** `lernwelt_big_text` (large text) and `lernwelt_easy_mode` (fewer quiz
  options) make it easier to start.

## 18. Little missions & ideas

- 🐝 **Pollinator:** brush-pollinate 5 flowers.
- 🍎 **Gardener:** grow an apple from a pollinated blossom and harvest it.
- 🍯 **Beekeeper:** extract your first honey from nectar.
- 🔢 **Counter:** count 10 honey drops at the filled comb.
- 📐 **Pattern master:** continue the honeycomb (hexagon) pattern at the honey-store board.
- 🎨 **Colour artist:** lay a flower row red-yellow-blue and solve the colour pattern.
- 🐞 **Animal friend:** help 3 ladybugs in the orchard.
- 📷 **Researcher:** photograph all animals (Discovered 13/13).
- 🌟 **Legend:** find the rare golden honey bee.

## 19. Troubleshooting

- **Animals don't appear automatically:** `lernwelt_spawn_creatures` must be on and a mob API is
  required (VoxeLibre/Mineclonia or `mobs_redo`). You can always place animals with **spawn eggs**.
- **The fruit doesn't grow:** you must **pollinate** the blossom with the **brush** first. Without
  pollination it stays a blossom.
- **The brush collects no nectar:** you must click a **flower** (red/yellow/blue …); only flowers
  give nectar.
- **The extractor does nothing:** you must hold **Nektar** (nectar).
- **The honeycomb is empty/grey:** it was just harvested — wait a moment, it **refills**.
- **No sound:** sound files are optional. Put your own `.ogg` files in the `sounds/` folder (see
  `sounds/README.txt`) — without them everything works, just silently.

Have fun pollinating, counting and laying patterns! 🐝🌸🍯
