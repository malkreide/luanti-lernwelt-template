# Player Guide — Tiefsee-Retter (Deep-Sea Rescue) 🐙🌊

This guide explains **all the elements** of the "Tiefsee-Retter" learning world,
**how to use them** and **what the tasks and goals are**. Made for **parents and
children playing together** (about 5–7 years old).

> Note: the in-game texts are in **German** (the theme is authored in German), so
> names you see on screen are German; this guide adds English explanations. German
> names appear with an English gloss where helpful.

> Motto: **Entdecken · Helfen · Bewahren** (Discover · Help · Protect)
>
> There is no "winning" — the point is to discover, help and learn: rescue and
> photograph animals, solve little tasks, keep the sea clean and grow a reef, while
> practising counting, patterns and naming (Swiss curriculum *Lehrplan 21*, cycle 1).

---

## 1. Quick start

1. Create a water-rich world first — see **[WELT-EINRICHTEN.md](WELT-EINRICHTEN.md)**
   (recommended: "flat sea").
2. Make a new world with **VoxeLibre** or **Mineclonia** and enable the mods
   **`lernwelt`** + **`lernwelt_tiefsee`**.
3. Enter the world → you automatically get the **starter kit**. Fast start: type the
   command **`/tiefsee_basis`** — a ready-made station with boards and a submarine
   appears in front of you.

---

## 2. Basic controls

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| **Use / rescue an animal / open a board** | **Right-click** |
| **Photograph / break litter / dig blocks** | **Left-click** (hold) |
| **Build / place an item** | **Right-click** on the ground |
| **Fly (creative)** | Double-jump; then jump = up, sneak = down |

In the child setting, **damage and drowning are off** — nothing bad can happen. The
time of day is fixed (no scary night), and there are no monsters.

---

## 3. The world: 4 learning zones

Each zone is a "habitat" with a **learning board** and matching animals:

| Zone | Animals there | Learning areas |
|---|---|---|
| 🪸 **Korallenriff** (coral reef) | Clownfish, Turtle, Seahorse, Crab | Maths, science, German |
| 🌊 **Offenes Meer** (open sea) | Blue whale, Shark, Dolphin, *Golden whale* | Maths, science, movement |
| 🌑 **Dunkle Tiefsee** (dark deep sea) | Anglerfish, Octopus | Science (light/dark) |
| ⏳ **Meeresboden** (sea floor) | Starfish, Jellyfish, Ray, Manatee | Maths, science, media/IT |

---

## 4. Learning boards & tasks (the heart of the game)

**Use:** right-click a learning board → a window shows the **activity**, the
**curriculum links** and the zone's **tasks**.

Three task types:

- ❓ **Quiz** — a question with answer buttons. Right = ✓ + reward, wrong = "try again".
- 🎨 **Copy the pattern** — a colour pattern (e.g. red-yellow-red) is shown at the top.
  Click the colour buttons **in the right order**. A mistake resets the pattern.
- 🐠 **Rescue goal** — e.g. "rescue 2 clownfish". The progress (x/N) counts up
  automatically as you rescue.

Solved tasks give a sound + praise and count towards your progress
(see `/lernfortschritt`).

**Which tasks are there?**

| Zone | Tasks |
|---|---|
| Coral reef | Quiz "clownfish colour" · Rescue 2 clownfish · Quiz "first letter of *Krabbe*" |
| Open sea | Quiz "who is bigger?" · Rescue 1 blue whale |
| Dark deep sea | Quiz "which animal glows?" |
| Sea floor | Quiz "how many arms has a starfish?" · Rescue 2 starfish · **pattern** red-yellow-red |

## 5. Rescuing animals 🐠

**Use:** right-click an animal = **rescue**. You get hearts, a sound, a counter entry
and (after enough rescues) a new rank.

**All animals:**

| Animal (DE / EN) | Home | Eats | Power | Note |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Clownfisch / Clownfish | Reef | Algae | Lives in anemones | |
| Schildkröte / Turtle | Reef | Sea grass | Very long-lived | 👪 family |
| Seepferdchen / Seahorse | Reef | Plankton | Swims upright | |
| Krabbe / Crab | Reef | Algae | Walks sideways | |
| Blauwal / Blue whale | Open sea | Krill | Huge & loud | |
| Hai / Shark | Open sea | Fish | Great sense of smell | friendly |
| Delfin / Dolphin | Open sea | Fish | Jumps & clicks | 👪 family |
| Anglerfisch / Anglerfish | Dark deep sea | Small fish | Glows | 💡 glows |
| Krake / Octopus | Dark deep sea | Crabs | Squirts ink | |
| Seestern / Starfish | Sea floor | Mussels | Arms grow back | |
| Qualle / Jellyfish | Sea floor | Plankton | Squishy | 💡 glows |
| Rochen / Ray | Sea floor | Mussels | Glides like a kite | |
| Seekuh / Manatee | Sea floor | Sea grass | Gentle & slow | 👪 family |
| **Goldener Wal / Golden whale** | Open sea | Sunlight | Rare & magical | 🌟 very rare, glows |

- 👪 **Families:** rescue a parent (Dolphin, Turtle, Manatee) and its **babies**
  appear, which you can rescue too — a lovely "help" moment.
- 🌟 **Golden whale:** does **not** appear by itself (very rare). Get it from its
  **spawn egg** (in the starter kit / creative inventory).
- 🌊 **Living sea:** the other water animals **appear automatically** in water near
  you (gently capped). Can be turned off (see Settings).

**Spawn eggs:** every animal has an egg. Egg in hand → **right-click** into water =
place the animal. That's how you summon animals to rescue.

---

## 6. Explorer camera 📷

**Use:** hold the camera, get near an animal and **left-click** = photo. The nearest
**not-yet-discovered** animal of this world is marked **discovered** in the logbook
(range about 12 blocks).

This is the **collecting game**: photograph **all** animals. The logbook shows
"Discovered: X/14". Discovering is separate from rescuing.

---

## 7. Sea logbook 📋

**Use:** logbook in hand → **right-click** to open.

You see a list of all animals; click one for the **profile** (name, habitat, eats,
power), how **often you rescued** it, and whether it has been **discovered**. The top
shows your **rank**, the **total rescues** and the **discovered count**.

---

## 8. Ranks & badges 🏆

Every rescue counts. At certain thresholds your rank goes up (sound + pop-up); from
"Junior-Retter" on you also get a **badge item** in your inventory (right-click it for
your tally).

| From … rescues | Rank | Badge |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | Frischling (Newcomer) | – |
| 5 | Tauchschüler (Diving student) | – |
| 10 | Junior-Retter (Junior rescuer) | 🥉 yes |
| 25 | Meeresheld (Sea hero) | 🥈 yes |
| 50 | Kapitän (Captain) | 🥇 yes |

Top-left a **HUD** always shows: world · rank · rescues.

---

## 9. Clean the sea 🧹 ("Protect")

Sometimes there is **litter** in the sea (bottle, can, bag). **Left-click** to collect
it → the sea gets cleaner, your own counter rises, and there are **milestones**:

| Litter collected | Title |
|---|---|
| 5 | Meeres-Putzer (Sea cleaner) |
| 15 | Riff-Retter (Reef saver) |
| 30 | Ozean-Held (Ocean hero) |

Get practice litter with the command `/tiefsee_muell` or from the test stations.

---

## 10. Plant corals 🪸 ("Protect")

**Use:** hold a **coral sapling** → **right-click** the ground = plant. After a short
time (about 20 s) it **grows** into a colourful coral. Each grown coral counts towards
your **reef counter** (sound + message). Build up your own reef piece by piece.

---

## 11. Submarine 🚤

**Use:**
1. **Place** the submarine item (right-click on the ground / into water).
2. **Right-click** the capsule = **get in**.
3. Drive: **W/S** forward/back · **A/D** steer · **jump** = up · **sneak** = down ·
   **right-click** = get out.

**Upgrades:**
- 🔦 **Headlight** — the capsule glows while someone is driving.
- 🫧 **Bubble trail** — bubbles rise while moving.
- 👨‍👧 **Second seat** — a **second player** can **ride along** with right-click
  (ideal for parent + child).

---

## 12. Blocks, items & internal names

Everything is generated without external pictures. Internal names (for the creative
search or `/giveme`) begin with `lernwelt_tiefsee:`.

**Blocks:** `koralle_rot/blau/gelb/pink/gruen` (glowing corals) · `stationsglas`
(transparent build glass) · `koralle_setzling` (grows into a coral) ·
`muell_flasche/dose/tuete` (litter) · `tafel_riff/offenes_meer/tiefsee/meeresboden`
(learning boards).

**Patterned build blocks:** `muster_streifen/punkte/schach/rahmen`, `schatzkiste`,
`sand_muschel`, `wellen`, `fischschuppen` (scales), `anker` (anchor), `steuerrad`
(ship's wheel), `truhe` (a real chest with a lid on top), and `buchstabe_a` … `buchstabe_z`
(**letter blocks A–Z** to lay out words). In creative mode all blocks are in the build menu.

**Items:** `logbuch` · `kamera` · `tauchkapsel` · the spawn eggs (e.g. `clownfisch`,
`delfin`, `goldwal`) · badges (`badge_10/25/50`).

> **Own patterns or pictures on blocks?** Yes! A block can use `texture = "..."`
> instead of `color = "#..."`:
> - **Pattern without a file** — layer colour rectangles, e.g.
>   `texture = "[fill:16x16:#3498db^[fill:8x8:0,0:#ffffff^[fill:8x8:8,8:#ffffff"`
>   (checker). Overlay format: `^[fill:WIDTHxHEIGHT:X,Y:#colour`.
> - **A real picture** — put a PNG (16×16 or 32×32) in the `textures/` folder and set
>   `texture = "lernwelt_tiefsee_mypic.png"`.
> - **Per face** — `top`, `side` and `bottom` give a block different faces (like a
>   chest). See `textures/README.txt`.

## 14. Settings

In the menu under **Settings → All settings → Mods** (or in `minetest.conf`):

| Setting | Default | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| `lernwelt_show_hud` | on | Progress display top-left |
| `lernwelt_spawn_creatures` | on | Animals appear automatically in water |
| `lernwelt_big_text` | off | **Larger text** on the learning boards |
| `lernwelt_easy_mode` | off | **Easy mode**: quizzes show only 2 answers |
| `lernwelt_tiefsee_starter_kit` | on | Starter kit on first join |

---

## 15. For parents & teachers 👪

- **Lehrplan 21 (cycle 1):** every zone and task is tagged with competence areas —
  Maths (counting, patterns, sizes), German (naming), science (animals & habitats,
  light/dark), media & informatics (sequences/patterns), movement.
- `/lernplan` shows all zones and their links at a glance.
- `/lernfortschritt` shows per child: rank, rescues, tasks solved, animals met and the
  learning areas covered.
- **Accessibility:** `lernwelt_big_text` (large text) and `lernwelt_easy_mode` (fewer
  quiz options) make it easier to start.

## 16. Little missions & ideas

- 🟢 **First steps:** rescue 5 animals → rank "Tauchschüler".
- 📷 **Researcher:** photograph all animals (Discovered 14/14).
- 🧩 **Puzzler:** solve all tasks on all four boards.
- 🧹 **Cleaner:** collect 15 litter → "Riff-Retter".
- 🪸 **Reef builder:** plant 10 corals.
- 🔤 **Word play:** lay your name with the letter blocks (`buchstabe_*`).
- 🌟 **Legend:** rescue the rare golden whale.
- 👨‍👧 **Team dive:** ride the submarine together.

## 17. Troubleshooting

- **Animals flop on land / disappear:** they need **water**. Go to the sea or build a
  pool (see WELT-EINRICHTEN.md).
- **No sound:** sound files are optional. Put your own `.ogg` files in the `sounds/`
  folder (see `sounds/README.txt`) — without them everything works, just silently.
- **"Unknown node":** if old blocks are missing, the engine ships compatibility
  aliases; tell us if something still shows up.
- **Animals don't appear automatically:** `lernwelt_spawn_creatures` must be on and a
  mob API is required (VoxeLibre/Mineclonia or `mobs_redo`). You can always place
  animals with **spawn eggs**.

Have fun discovering, helping and protecting! 🐙🌊
